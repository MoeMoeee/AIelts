Revised:

These days, millions of youngsters are enrolling in universities every year. Some people believe that young people should consider taking a job for a few years between school and university instead of continuing their tertiary education immediately after high school. This essay will discuss the advantages and disadvantages of taking a gap year.

On the one hand, pursuing higher education requires a significant amount of money. Therefore, some young individuals choose to work in order to save enough money to cover their first year of university expenses, such as tuition fees, living costs, and books. Having saved money allows them to focus more on their academic studies without worrying about their financial situation. Consequently, they are able to achieve better academic results. Furthermore, during their time in the workforce, individuals often acquire valuable soft skills, such as time management and communication skills, which can enhance their academic performance.

On the other hand, it is frequently observed that many young people lose their motivation to continue studying after spending some time working. The desire to learn often decreases slightly after being in the workforce. A recent survey revealed that one in three individuals in their twenties never return to higher education after taking a gap year. Consequently, they face a higher risk of unemployment in the future. Even in cases where individuals decide to resume their studies after a couple of years of working, their ability to absorb new information and learn may become slower. Ultimately, this can have a negative impact on their overall academic achievements.

In conclusion, while some individuals prefer to work for a year after high school graduation, it is important to consider both the advantages and disadvantages. Working during this time can provide opportunities to earn money and acquire skills, but it may also lead to a decrease in motivation to pursue higher education. Additionally, some individuals may never return to university after taking a gap year.
