It is often believed that high-end technology can effectively prevent and reduce the rate of crimes in society. This essay agrees with the notion that living in a technologically advanced era can play a significant role in minimizing criminal activities. To begin with, modern technology has provided us with various safety and security features through mobile applications. Therefore, it is evident that technology plays a crucial role in reducing crime rates.

The presence of these cameras makes potential offenders more cautious and less likely to engage in illegal activities, due to the fear of getting caught. For example, in Bangalore, crime rates have witnessed a substantial decrease of 20 percent following the installation of surveillance cameras in street markets. Thus, the adoption of modern cameras developed with advanced technology proves to be an effective method in minimizing criminal activities.

In conclusion, it is evident that living in the contemporary era of computer technology offers numerous advantages in preventing crimes. By utilizing features such as mapping technology in mobile phones and installing surveillance cameras in public areas, criminal offenses can be significantly reduced.
