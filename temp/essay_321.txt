Topic:

"Your English-speaking friend who lives in your town has asked for your advice about learning a new sport.

Write an email to your friend.

•Recommend a new sport that would be suitable for your friend to learn

•Explain how your friend could learn this sport

•Suggest that you both learn this sport together."

Essay:

"Dear Jahid,

I hope this letter finds you best in health and spirits. I am writing you this letter to give you my suggestion for learning a suitable sport, basketball. It is a sport which can keep you very fit and healthy.

To learn to play basketball, every day after work come to Wellesley Court, which is 10 minutes far from both of our places. We can start over with some basic drills. Building stamina is essential for this sport therefore to build stamina we can take 10 laps of the whole court. Afterwards, we can do some dribbling drills around the whole court to make ourselves comfortable running with the ball. And finally, we can take some shots around the whole court. 

Once we are good with this first startup practice, we can start playing a 1 on 1 match to get easier with the basic rules. Practicing this sport with each other can be beneficial for both of us in terms of staying fit and learning a new sport.

I am sure you will absolutely like this sport. I am hoping to hear back from you soon. Hope we can start the new sport by next week. Please let me know your convenient time after work.

Best Regards,

Nazmul"


Revised:

Dear Jahid,

I hope this letter finds you in good health and high spirits. I am writing to offer my suggestion for a suitable sport for you to learn: basketball. Not only is it a fun and exciting sport, but it also helps to keep you fit and healthy.

To begin your journey in basketball, I recommend that we meet at Wellesley Court, which is conveniently located just 10 minutes away from both of our places. We can start with some basic drills to familiarize ourselves with the fundamental skills required in basketball. It is important to focus on building stamina, so we can start by taking 10 laps of the court. This will help improve our endurance. 

After that, we can move on to practicing dribbling drills around the court. This will help us become more comfortable running with the ball. Lastly, we can work on our shooting skills by taking shots from different spots around the court.

Once we have mastered these basic skills, we can then progress to playing a one-on-one match. This will help us become more familiar with the rules of the game and improve our overall playing abilities. 

Learning and practicing this sport together will not only benefit us in terms of staying fit, but it will also give us the opportunity to bond and have fun. 

I am confident that you will thoroughly enjoy playing basketball. I am eager to hear back from you soon, and I hope that we can start learning this new sport together by next week. Please let me know your availability after work.

Best regards,

Nazmul
