Revised:

People affected by stress-related illnesses are rapidly increasing. This essay will discuss the two major factors that contribute to pressure-related sickness and provide suggestions to overcome them.

Two vital areas that are a source of stress are work and family. Many individuals are unhappy with their work or employer, despite spending most of their lifetime in the workplace. This naturally leads to stress-related illnesses. For instance, employees with a poor manager will always face higher levels of stress compared to workers under a reputable manager. Additionally, family dynamics play an influential role in creating stress. Constant altercations between spouses can significantly increase stress levels. For example, partners going through a divorce or on the brink of separation will suffer from mental pressure compared to those who have a peaceful family life. This emphasizes that an improper work environment and poor family relationships can contribute to stress among individuals.

Finding a suitable job and seeking help to improve family relationships will undoubtedly reduce mental and physical struggles. Instead of enduring a miserable job throughout one's lifespan, it is crucial to find fulfilling employment. In order to relieve stress, a close friend of mine gave up his software development work and switched careers to teaching. Additionally, seeking the assistance of a therapist can help improve family relationships. Although couples can communicate with each other to resolve disputes, they should not hesitate to seek external help. Stress can be mitigated to a great extent by finding a fulfilling job and seeking help for relationship issues.

In conclusion, in the modern world, an increasing number of people are becoming ill as a result of stress. This essay discussed how stress is caused by unhappiness at work and weak family relationships. Moreover, it suggested that finding a better job and reaching out to a therapist for family issues can help protect individuals from stress.
