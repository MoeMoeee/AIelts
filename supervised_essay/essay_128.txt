Topic:

"Concern for the environment is growing and more and more people are choosing to recycle. But some people suggest that recycling is not enough. They believe we need to consume less.
To what extent do you agree or disagree?"

Essay:

"Over the time more and more people started to recycle in order to preserve and protect the environment together with his natural resources.However some people believe that we need to focus more on the amount of waste we produce every day.
To begin with we have recycle bins everywhere with different colours for different products which they vary from paper to glass.Moreover,recycling helps reducing the volume of rubbish sent to landfill sites where the rubbish is buried in a way that it will be out of contact with the air.
Nevertheless, some people think we need to pay more attention to the waste that we are producing.For instance ,supermarkets should stop using promotions that tempt us into buying more than we need. It's essential that the waste is handled , stored , transported and disposed of properly.
In conclusion, we should focus much more on the load of waste we are producing than on the quantity of trash we are recycling."


Revised:

Concern for the environment is increasing, and recycling has become a popular solution to reduce waste. However, some argue that recycling alone is not enough and that we need to consume less. This topic is important in the current context, and there are different views on the best course of action to reduce our impact on the environment.

On the one hand, recycling is an effective way to reduce waste and preserve natural resources. Many countries have implemented recycling programs, and people are encouraged to recycle by the availability of recycling bins and government campaigns. Furthermore, recycling reduces the amount of garbage sent to landfills, which helps to control pollution and methane emissions.

On the other hand, some believe that recycling is not enough, as the amount of waste we produce is still too high. They argue that we need to consume less by changing our behaviors and habits, for instance, by avoiding disposable plastic products, reducing food waste, and using public transport instead of private cars. Additionally, manufacturers and supermarkets should use eco-friendly materials and packaging and avoid promoting overconsumption.

In conclusion, the concern for the environment is driving change in our daily habits, and recycling has become a popular measure to protect the planet. However, we need to go beyond recycling and consume less to reduce our impact on the environment. This essay has highlighted the importance of both recycling and reducing consumption and their potential contribution to preserve the planet's natural resources.
