Topic:

"Some people fail in school, but end up being successful in life.



Why do you think that is the case?



What is the most important thing to succeed in life"

Essay:

"There is a typical case that someone who got a terrible score in school obtained remarkable achievements in their life. I think that finding a favourite job is the key to success, which directly causes this situation mentioned before. 



On the one hand, we have to recognize that customized subjects in school provide students with fewer choices, leading them to hardly dig out their actual interests. For instance, a high school always teach   teenagers some normal lessons such as math, history, science, and so on. Meanwhile, students struggle in getting good scores in the examination and thus fail to explore the thing which matches their interests and talent. Therefore, it is reasonable that some people got a lower rank in school.



On the other hand, society offers people more chance to seek their preferred job and career, which bring those who failed in their school life success. When people find things they are interested in, they tend to gain significant energy and self-satisfaction, making them take a giant step in life. Take finding a job as an example, people who are satisfied with their current job have a greater possibility of getting promoted and a big salary increment than those who lose interest in their job since they are willing to work hard in the company. Hence, people who get a favourite job and work in a suitable atmosphere tend to be successful.



Although some factors are able to create success in people's life, I consider that gaining the favourite position is the most important thing to succeed in life"


Revised:

There are instances where individuals may perform poorly in school but later achieve great success in life. I believe that finding a passion or a job that one truly enjoys is the key to this phenomenon.

On one hand, it is important to recognize that schools often offer limited choices in terms of subjects and fields of study. Students are generally required to study a set curriculum which may not align with their true interests. Consequently, they may struggle to excel academically and fail to explore their true potential and talents. Hence, it is not surprising that some individuals do not perform well in school.

On the other hand, society provides ample opportunities for individuals to pursue their preferred careers and passions. When people find jobs or activities that genuinely interest them, they tend to find a sense of fulfillment and motivation, thereby propelling them to achieve remarkable success in life. For example, when individuals are satisfied and engaged in their jobs, they are more likely to work diligently and effectively, resulting in career advancement and financial rewards. Hence, those who find their preferred jobs and work in a conducive environment are more likely to achieve success.

In conclusion, while some people may struggle academically, they can still attain great success in life by finding a job or pursuing activities that they truly love. This is crucial as it provides individuals with a sense of purpose and enables them to unleash their full potential. Ultimately, finding one's passion is the most important factor in achieving success in life.
