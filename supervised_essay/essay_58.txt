Revised:

In recent times, an increasing number of individuals with health-related issues are turning to alternative methods of treatment, such as herbal remedies, instead of consulting their health expert. While these treatments can be helpful at times, it is a negative development as they lack prescription, the manufacturers have little or no adequate knowledge, and the treatment areas do not have necessary equipment.

To begin with, orthodox methods of treatment and drug supply have many dangers attached to them despite being affordable. The lack of proper dosage information can lead to individuals taking more or less than the required amount, leading to various conditions and even death. Additionally, those who carry out these treatments or produce these drugs often have little or no relevant knowledge as they did not undergo formal training, leading them to rely on guesswork and putting human lives at risk.

Moreover, these treatment areas are likely to lack necessary equipment to carry out specific procedures, jeopardizing the lives of patients. For instance, traditional bone setters attempt to manipulate bones without the aid of X-ray machines, leading to severe complications, limb loss, and even death.

In conclusion, while people may lack the financial resources to access good health facilities and buy quality drugs, it is crucial to utilize the available resources properly by visiting reputable hospitals. The government has made free healthcare facilities available to avoid drug abuse and ensure proper treatment by trained healthcare professionals, avoiding the dangers associated with alternative treatments.
