"Some people argue that capital punishment is good for a country. To what extent do you agree or
disagree?"

Essay:

"Crime rates have been on the rise in the past decades, despite the culprit aware of the consequences he shall face for the offence committed by him.It is often argued that, capital punishment is beneficial for the well being of the nation and the citizens attached to it.I strongly concur with the fact that capital punishment should be improvised.

To begin with, punishments are essential for maintaining peace throughout the country.People are familiar with the law and order of their nation and if the law is broken, it usually means that injustice has taken place and harmony is being disturbed.Punishing a culprit makes the fear vanish from the minds of innocent people.For instance, Ajmal Kasab, a terrorist was killed in the attacks and peace was finally restored.

On the other hand, offence rates define the character of a country. Having said this, no matter how developed or developing a country is, if the crimes committed are increasing every year it reflects a bad character of the country to the world. This leads to the downfall of the economy and many other global factors are affected. For example, tourists avoid such places where they don't feel safe or places which have a bad image in the world.This ultimately leads to the loss of economy. Hence, executing a criminal for a harsh crime and preparing several punishments according to the weightage of crimes should be done.

To summarise, I strongly believe that capital punishments should be a part of law and order of the country as they will surely help maintaining peace, harmony and bring discipline to the people in the country."

Revised:

In recent years, crime has become a widespread issue globally, despite stricter laws and severe consequences that offenders may face. Some people claim that capital punishment is beneficial for the well-being of a country. I agree that improving the capital punishment system would help to curb crime rates and maintain social order.

To begin with, punishment plays a crucial role in maintaining peace throughout the country. When an individual is aware of the consequences of criminal activities, it creates a sense of fear and deters them from committing a criminal act. As a result, it can prevent potential offenders from committing violent crimes. For instance, in the case of Ajmal Kasab, a terrorist who was executed for his crimes, the act acted as a deterrent to similar attacks and contributed to maintaining peace in society.

Moreover, high crime rates can tarnish a country's reputation and discourage investors and tourists from visiting or investing, leading to a loss of economic opportunities. Therefore, it is essential to address crime rates and present a positive image of the country. The implementation of severe punishments such as capital punishment for serious crimes can reduce the crime rate by deterring potential offenders and promoting a feeling of safety in society.

In conclusion, I firmly believe that the capital punishment system should be improved and integrated into the law and order of the country. It promotes discipline and social order by showing the consequences of committing a crime. This helps to maintain peace and prevent the negativity that a high crime rate can bring to society. It is a practical solution for maintaining law and order in a country while simultaneously enhancing its reputation.
