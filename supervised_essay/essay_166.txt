Topic:

"Is freedom of speech necessary in a free society? Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience."

Essay:

"Nowadays, people prefer a society where they can express themselves openly, which helps to build a better nation in many ways. In this essay, we will look at the reasons which support the statement. 

Firstly, it is always necessary to maintain fairness for everyone in the country. Most of the time we listen to the government on the way they are improving the country. At times, the ideas given by elected personnel will not blend well with the public. This is where society needs to have an avenue on providing their thoughts. In addition, this will help to build equality, where everyone contributes to the betterment of a nation. For instance, in my state, a major government initiative often gets pointed out by netizens on social media, which at times is seen to be logical and will be good if the ideas are implemented. 

Secondly, the public will have the freedom to point on the wrongdoings of individuals including officials without fear of being criminalized. This further helps to create a better society to live in where people will blend in well with authorities such as the police. In addition, government officials will tend to perform their duties better as they can be called out by the public for their mistakes. For instance, in my country, the police department gets frequent feedback from the public on the way they treat criminals, which helps them to implement better policies. Nevertheless, individuals who openly speaks on current issues can be identified as future leaders of the nation, where they will have an understanding of society and strategies towards the betterment of it. 

In conclusion, a nation should allow its citizens to speak freely on issues related to the country and accept the criticism so that it will provide benefits that will help to build a better community. This allows the people of the nation to live peacefully where equal treatment is given for all."

Revised:

In today's society, people recognize the importance of expressing oneself freely which can contribute to the development of the nation. In this essay, we will explore the reasons that support the statement that freedom of speech is essential in a free society.

Firstly, it is crucial to ensure fairness in a country. Although the government may present their ideas to improve the country, people may not always agree with them. Therefore, it is necessary to have a platform where people's opinions can be heard and considered. This promotes equality and enables all individuals to contribute to the betterment of their nation. For example, on social media, people in my state often provide logical insights into major government initiatives that need improvements.

Secondly, freedom of speech allows individuals to criticize wrongful acts committed by anyone without fear of being criminalized. It fosters a harmonious coexistence between the people and the authorities such as the police. Moreover, it forces the officials to perform better and avoid making mistakes as they can be held accountable by the public. For instance, the police department in my country frequently receives feedback from people about their treatment of criminals, resulting in better policies. Furthermore, individuals who speak up about current issues may become future leaders of the nation, retaining a deep understanding of society and new strategies to improve it.

In conclusion, a nation should enable its people to express their thoughts freely concerning issues related to their country. Through accepting constructive criticisms, a better society can be achieved. This allows people to live in peace and prosperity, knowing that equal treatment is given to all.
