Topic:

"Some people think that newly built houses should follow the style of old houses in local areas. Others think that people should have freedom to build houses of their own style. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion."

Essay:

"There are those who think that people who make houses newly have to comply with the traditional style in municipal areas, whereas others consider that they have to design their own residences by themselves. Deciding whether or not it is wise to own style is subjective but for many it can be positive. 

On the one hand, if new citizens follow the conventional custom to create their dwellings, it would render the scenery of the town beautiful. To be specific, it would make the local area more famous in the country. For instance, one city in Kyoto decided a rule of building to oblige the traditional form in 2018, it generated some positive effects on the residents and town because it became the most popular area in Japan in 2020. Thus, this trend would produce some beneficial results. 

on the other hand, a lot of individuals build their own dwellings since they want to live in their favorite houses. It means that house owners design what they would like to do in their own residences. To illustrate, many families attach a garden in their houses to fulfill their hope such as playing with their children or watching nature. Hence, if newcomers are able to erect their home freely, they can design what they like. 

In my view, it depends on what house the person like to live in. There is no specific residence that fits everyone, and I think if individuals think that harmony with other residents is more essential than their favorites, they should comply the rule of old house, and vice versa. 

In conclusion, there are pros and cons for traditional custom and own style. It depends on the individual’s choice. Therefore, people have to consider what they want to obtain from their own buildings."


Revised:

It is a widely debated topic whether people constructing new houses should adhere to traditional styles prevalent in the local areas or have the freedom to design their homes as per their liking. In this essay, we will explore both these views and present our opinion.

On the one hand, adhering to the traditional styles would give the local area a more aesthetically pleasing appearance. When new buildings follow the local architecture, it creates a sense of cohesion within the community, preserving its cultural heritage. A recent example is seen in Kyoto, where the authorities mandated that all new constructions must follow traditional forms. This has resulted in the area's popularity soaring. Thus, following traditional styles comes with its advantages.

On the other hand, many people prefer to design their own homes according to their personal taste. Homeowners should have autonomy over their own dwelling, especially if it’s their lifelong dream. Such freedom leads to houses that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. For instance, many people integrate gardens into their homes to fulfill their desires and enjoy nature's tranquility. Therefore, it is important to grant people the ability to design their own homes if they wish to do so.

In our opinion, while it is commendable to preserve the local cultural heritage through traditional styles, people should also have the freedom to design their ideal homes. Individuals may want to express their unique style or exceed their daily needs, which may not be possible with traditional homes. Thus, the decision should depend on what the homeowner wants to achieve from the house they build.

In conclusion, both traditional style and the freedom to design houses have their advantages and disadvantages. However, individuals should be allowed to make their own decisions according to their subjective preferences and what they want to gain from their homes. Only then can one have homes that not only meet their needs but also reflect their choices and personal styles.
