"Marketing and promotion is a key to successful business? To what extent do you agree?"

So, companies from the lower scale to affluent companies are spending a lot of money in advertising. I do not completely accord with this point because quality and purity products would popular automatically.

On the other flip side of the coin, few brands are not required any promotion but still they would more popular with their quality and cost. If a product or company has to be successful, it should be capable of making best quality and unique masterpiece for the world which automatically brings huge publicity all over the world.

In conclusion, marketing is one of the best and easiest ways to bring success for the company but without any advertisements also we can get success as well."


Revised:

In today’s fast-paced world, marketing and promotion have become indispensable tools for businesses. It is widely believed that pouring huge amounts of money into advertising is the key to success. However, I partly disagree with this view as I believe that the quality and uniqueness of products can attract customers without the need for extensive advertising.

On the one hand, mesmerizing advertisements have the potential to attract a large number of customers to stores. Moreover, these advertisements help to raise awareness about the products and their uses among people who are not well-versed in the field. Subsequently, people tend to show an inclination towards brands even if they are expensive. This, in turn, fuels the growth of large-scale businesses, enabling them to reap the benefits of increased demand. For instance, companies like Nike, Puma, and Rolex have gained immense popularity worldwide due to their promotional activities.

On the other hand, some products do not require extensive promotion and still manage to gain a significant amount of popularity solely based on their quality and unique features. For instance, Lamborgini cars are highly sought after and are considered to be a dream car for many people, despite not being advertised on television. Companies that focus on producing high-quality and unique products are highly likely to gain popularity without having to spend a fortune on advertising.

In conclusion, marketing and promotion can be effective tools to achieve success for a company. However, it is not always necessary to rely solely on advertising as the quality and uniqueness of a product can generate substantial publicity on its own. Therefore, a balance between quality products and effective marketing strategies can bring about long-term success for a business.
