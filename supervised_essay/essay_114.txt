Nowadays, some children have an unhealthy lifestyle, and both schools and parents are responsible for finding solutions. In this essay, the impact of family and schools on children's health will be discussed.

To begin, the starting point of an unhealthy lifestyle is largely dependent on how much care children take of themselves. If they do not prioritize their health, they engage in activities that harm their well-being, such as spending excessive time on computers, having unbalanced diets, developing addictions, and not getting enough sleep. Parents play a crucial role in promoting their children's health, as they serve as role models, and their behavior often influences their children's habits. Therefore, parents' decisions, such as diet plans and sleep routines, significantly impact their children's lifestyle, including their physical and mental health. Additionally, family decisions such as divorce can impact children's psychology and have long-term negative effects on their overall health.

Schools also play an instrumental role in shaping children's lifestyles, both positively and negatively. Schools can affect children's mental health negatively by creating additional stress, pressure related to exams and future-thinking, and social problems with the formation of friendships. On the other hand, schools can positively impact children's lifestyle with classes focusing on wellness and fitness or culinary-related activities, which help in building good dietary habits. Such courses also contribute to children's future life planning, and may potentially affect their career choices.

In conclusion, creating a healthy lifestyle for children rests on the effort of parents, schools, and the children themselves. It is important for parents to serve as role models and instill good habits in their children. Similarly, schools should offer classes that can encourage healthy lifestyles and positive mental and physical health. However, the personal conscience and actions of children play the most significant role in promoting a healthy lifestyle that will ensure good health and well-being in the long run.
