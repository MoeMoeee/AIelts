Topic:

"Some people prefer to spend their lives doing the same things and avoiding change. Others, however, think that the change is always a good thing. Discuss both the views and give your own opinion. Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant example from your knowledge or experience."

Essay:

"People are different from each other in many ways. One such difference is the way of spending life. While some people like to spend their life doing one job or similar routine, others prefer changes in their lives. In my opinion a continuous change in life is the way to live. In the following essay we will discuss both views.
A famous saying is “never drink from stagnant river”. Firstly, some people get bored of same routine. They need change that can either be in job or daily routine. This helps them to take their mind off certain things and explore better options for themselves. For example it is a common trend among Irish people to seek for jobs overseas. This helps them in learning the life better and find better opportunities for themselves. Secondly, some people resort to travelling and living abroad for extended periods of time. Their idea is to take a time off from the same routine and spend some time in foreign culture and understanding different ways of life. For instance many government servants take time off after retirement to travel and enjoy their lives.
On the other hand, some people prefer to follow a strict routine. This is their way of life which has discipline. Such people are generally more career focused and cannot take the risk of discontinuity. For example, If we glance at lives of Buddha monks, their way of life is everything to them. Following strict routines help them to be strong. Similarly many sportsperson follow same routine of working certain hours of the day and focusing on health in the rest of time. Hence the choice depends on individuals thinking.
In conclusion, both views have their own advantages and disadvantages, but most of it depends on personal choices and preferences. In my opinion traveling and continuous change is the way to live in the world as it gives us more chance to explore the world and find better option for our careers."

Revised:

Here is the revised essay, adhering to the IELTS structure:

People have different preferences when it comes to how they live their lives. While some individuals prefer to stick to a familiar routine and avoid change, others believe that change is always a positive thing. In my opinion, a life characterized by continuous change is the most fulfilling. In the following essay, we will discuss both views.

Firstly, some people find themselves becoming bored and stagnant when following the same routine for an extended period of time. For such individuals, change, whether it be in their job or daily routine, provides an opportunity to refresh their perspective and explore new options. For example, it is common for Irish people to seek employment overseas. By immersing themselves in a new environment and exploring different opportunities, they can gain a better understanding of life while also discovering better career prospects.

Secondly, there are those who choose to embrace change by traveling and living abroad for extended periods of time. By breaking away from their usual routine, they can broaden their horizons, immerse themselves in foreign cultures, and gain a deeper appreciation for different ways of life. This is particularly evident when retired government officials take the opportunity to travel and enjoy their lives after many years of adhering to a strict routine.

On the other hand, there are individuals who prefer to follow a strict routine throughout their lives. For them, maintaining discipline and structure is paramount. This approach is commonly observed among Buddhist monks, whose entire lives revolve around adhering to strict routines. Similarly, many athletes follow specific training schedules and dedicate themselves to maintaining their physical well-being. It is important to acknowledge that this preference for stability and routine varies from person to person and is often driven by individual goals and aspirations.

In conclusion, both viewpoints have their own advantages and disadvantages, and the choice largely depends on personal preferences. However, in my opinion, embracing change and adopting a lifestyle characterized by continuous exploration and adaptability is the ideal way to live. By embracing change, we open ourselves up to a myriad of opportunities, allowing us to discover and pursue better options in our personal and professional lives.
