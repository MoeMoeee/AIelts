In my opinion, it is important to strike a balance between work and leisure activities for teenagers. Part-time work can provide them with valuable life experiences and teach them important lessons about responsibility and discipline. However, it should not come at the expense of their education and overall well-being. Teenagers should be allowed to work part-time during certain times, such as school breaks or weekends, to earn some money and gain work experience. This way, they can still enjoy their recreational activities and focus on their studies during the regular school term.

To conclude, the issue of whether teenagers should work part-time or prioritize their rest and after-school activities is a matter of debate.
