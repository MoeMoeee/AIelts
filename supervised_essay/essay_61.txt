Topic:

"Some people say that professional workers such as doctors, nurses and teachers who make greater contributions to the society should be paid more than those people in the field of sports and entertainment. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"

Essay:

"Whether giving doctors, nurses and teachers higher salaries than sportsmen and entertainers is a hot debate in society. Despite the superstars also making plenty of contributions, professional workers definitely work more so that they deserve to earn more.

Doctors and nurses always work in the front line, which makes them sacrifice their own time with their families and even their lives ,especially the emergency moment. As we all know, the pandemic last few years brought us trouble. As we stayed at home and complained about the lockdown, doctors and nurses were working with the high chance to get the virus. They  might lose their lives.In conclusion, they deserve to get more money. 

Teaching requires high responsibilities and  patients. Teachers need to keep learning all the time, then they can cultivate a better generation. It is not an easy job and it needs more intelligent people to do it.  If the pay is lower than superstars, students might give less respect for this job,which is able to lead  to a shortage of teachers in the future. 

To sum up, professionals are not only responsible to the individuals now,for example, doctors and nurses save people from dangerous situations, but also are obliged to the society's future. Fewer people will contribute to our society if there are not enough teachers."


Revised:

Here's an edited version, following the IELTS structure guidelines:

Introduction:
The issue of whether professionals like doctors, nurses, and teachers deserve higher salaries than sports and entertainment personalities is a subject of heated debate. While some argue that athletes and entertainers also make significant contributions, I believe that the former group should receive higher pay due to the critical role they play in society.

Body Paragraph 1: 
Doctors and nurses are essential workers who often have to work in emergency situations and even risk their lives. During times of crisis like the COVID-19 pandemic, doctors and nurses are on the front lines, putting themselves at great risk to treat patients. As such, their sacrifices and contributions are immeasurable, and they deserve higher salaries to reflect the importance of their work.

Body Paragraph 2:
Teachers play a vital role in shaping the future through educating and training the next generation. Their job requires patience, skill, and dedication, and they must continually improve their craft to create a better society. If the pay for teaching is lower than that of sports and entertainment, it can lead to a shortage of teachers in the future, which would have serious implications for our society.

Conclusion:
In conclusion, while athletes and entertainers might also contribute greatly to society, the contributions of doctors, nurses, and teachers are essential for the wellbeing and future of our communities. Therefore, they deserve higher salaries to reflect the importance of their work. In short, by providing incentives and recognition, society can encourage more people to pursue these critical professions, ultimately leading to a better future for us all.
