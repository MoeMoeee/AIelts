"Humans are greedy creatures. Nothing will be enough for them. Although, it is a good quality to possess. And they want to achieve more and more heights in their life. So, being ambitious is significantly important for everyone. They can surpass their own limit and prove their worth. However, being too ambitious in life also detrimental to them. In this essay, I will delineate its importance as well as its benefits and drawbacks.

Revised:

Humans are inherently ambitious creatures, always striving to achieve more and surpass their own limits. While ambition is often regarded as a positive quality, it is important to examine its significance and weigh its benefits and drawbacks. This essay will delve into the importance of ambition and evaluate its positive and negative characteristics. 

Having a clear goal or ambition to aspire towards is crucial for leading a purposeful life. Without a sense of direction, one's life may be akin to running aimlessly, achieving nothing. Unlike animals, humans possess the ability to envision and pursue goals, which sets them apart. Moreover, when individuals are determined and persistent in their pursuit of success, no matter how small or significant, it boosts their morale and confidence. The satisfaction derived from accomplishing their ambition reinforces their sense of self-worth. 

However, there are both advantages and disadvantages associated with having ambition. On one hand, having an ambition to secure one's future is commendable and can lead to personal growth and prosperity. When individuals strive to attain their ambitions and succeed, it brings about positive consequences for themselves and those around them. On the other hand, channeling excessive amounts of time and energy into an ambition can be detrimental. This is particularly true when one spends precious years fruitlessly pursuing an unrealistic or unattainable dream. Additionally, being overly ambitious can negatively impact an individual's well-being and strain familial relationships. 

In conclusion, ambition is an integral part of human nature, propelling individuals to accomplish great things. It provides a sense of purpose and drives personal growth and development. However, it is essential to strike a balance and consider the potential consequences of excessive ambition. Pursuing an ambition should not come at the expense of one's well-being or happiness. As with anything, moderation and realistic expectations are key when harnessing the power of ambition.
