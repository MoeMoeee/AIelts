It is believed by some that modern technology can effectively reduce the occurrence of crimes in society. This essay supports the notion that technology can indeed help combat crimes. Secondly, the installation of surveilence cameras in urban areas, especially in areas with high footfall such as shopping centres, traffic junctions and markets, has proven to deter offenders from committing crimes. The presence of cameras has instilled fear into the minds of potential criminals, making them more reluctant to engage in illegal activities. For example, installing cameras led to a 20 percent reduction in crime rate in street markets in Bangalore. Therefore, the use of advanced surveillance cameras can contribute significantly to reducing the crime rate in any given area.

In conclusion, it is evident that computer technology can aid in the prevention and reduction of crimes in modern times, by using applications capable of tracking our locations and sharing them for our safety. Moreover, the use of advanced surveillance cameras can help curb criminal activities by instilling fear in potential perpetrators. Therefore, it is universally apparent that technology can play a credible role in mitigating crime rates.
