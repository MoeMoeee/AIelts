Revised:

Here is the edited version of the essay:

In modern societies, an increasing number of couples are deciding to have children later in life. There are various reasons behind this trend and some solutions can be considered to address this issue.

Financial issues can be a significant obstacle for couples when considering expanding their family. Nowadays, many newly-wed couples do not have financial stability, which makes it difficult for them to plan for a child. However, this problem can be controlled by governments by implementing effective approaches. For example, governments can motivate couples by offering child benefits such as financial assistance to become homeowners.

Another reason for couples postponing having children is the responsibility that comes with being parents. Many modern-day couples prefer to spend their time together and enjoy life rather than taking on the responsibility of raising children. To tackle this hurdle, promoting a culture that values parenthood can have a significant effect on the mindset of young couples. Furthermore, governments can incentivize adolescents to become parents by using various methods such as media campaigns and supporting newly-wed couples.

In conclusion, while financial issues and the responsibility of raising children can be significant obstacles to couples in deciding when to have children, there are solutions to these problems. The primary responsibility to address these issues lies with governments, who can provide support to couples both financially and through campaigns to promote parenthood as a positive life choice.

Grammatical Range and Accuracy:

Chào bạn, dưới đây là đánh giá chi tiết về Grammatical Range and Accuracy trong bài viết của bạn:

- Bạn sử dụng các thì đúng và thể động từ đa dạng.

    Ví dụ: "One of the most significant key that governments can apply, is motivating couples..."
    Trong câu này, từ "key" phải được sửa thành "keys" để phù hợp với chủ ngữ "one of the most significant keys".
    Điều này sẽ giúp bài viết của bạn trở nên chính xác hơn và dễ hiểu hơn cho người đọc.

- Bạn cần chú ý đến các lỗi ngữ pháp như sai cấu trúc câu và sai thể động từ.

    Ví dụ: "this couples’ decision", nên viết là "these couples' decisions", vì "couples" là số nhiều.

    Ví dụ: "Despite the mentioned factor, governments can control this trouble by employing some effective approaches". Trong câu này, nên sử dụng từ "issue" thay vì "trouble" vì "trouble" thường được sử dụng trong những trường hợp tiêu cực hơn.
