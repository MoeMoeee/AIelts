Here's an edited version of your essay with an IELTS structure:

Introduction
In today's modern world, online shopping has become increasingly popular, providing people with the convenience of shopping from the comfort of their own homes. However, some argue that the growth of online shopping will lead to the closing of all physical shops and stores in towns and cities. In this essay, I will express my opinion on whether I agree or disagree with this statement and provide relevant examples to support my argument.

Body Paragraph 1
Online shopping has undoubtedly made life more convenient and efficient for many people. With the click of a button, shoppers can purchase almost anything without ever having to leave their homes. Furthermore, online shopping has also made lives easier for introverts who may not enjoy interacting with sales staff personnel in a physical store. Traffic and parking issues, extensive physical activity for elderly people, and crowded outlets are other factors that deter people from visiting physical stores. All of these factors combined contribute to the rise in popularity of virtual marketplaces and online stores.

Body Paragraph 2
One reason why physical shops and stores may close due to online shopping is the cost to benefit ratio. Running a physical shop or store requires extensive costs such as rent, lighting, music, staff, and interior. These costs remain fixed even if there is a low number of incoming customers. In contrast, managing an online store is cheaper, as it provides flexibility in service. Customers can visit a virtual store, make purchases, and complaints can be handled through bots providing enhanced customer service. A lack of substantial fixed costs enables online stores to generate higher profits.

Body Paragraph 3
One real-life example of physical stores closing due to online shopping is Forever 21, a clothing brand that has closed its physical stores and is now focusing solely on online sales. Furthermore, Amazon and Ali Express are two major tech giants that have transformed the way people shop. These two companies alone have disrupted the idea of having physical stores. 

Conclusion
In conclusion, online shopping has become more popular than physical stores, and it is true that the growth of online shopping may lead to the closing of all shops and stores in the future. Therefore, brands should adapt to this changing environment, or they will be left behind like Blackberry when touchscreen smartphones came along. However, despite the advantages of online shopping, physical stores still play a crucial role in society as they provide people with a sense of community and opportunity to interact with others.
