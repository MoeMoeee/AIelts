Topic:

"Extreme sports such as sky diving and skiing are very dangerous and should be banned.

To what extent do you agree or disagree with this view?

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience."

Essay:

"It is believed that dangerous activities such as skiing and diving are threatening and should be brought to a stop. In this essay I shall share my thoughts and opinions regarding the topic.

Firstly, let's discuss as to why these sports are considered to be risky. One of the reasons that comes first to mind is that taking part in such activities which are highly dependent on related equipment which are not necessarily always reliable. Faults in devices used for such sports could even lead on to be fatal. Though backup equipment might be present for the user to make use of in case of an emergency, it needn't be one hundred per cent reliable cause it depends on how quick the user is to react to emergency situations. He or she should be able to demonstrate good presence of mind and agility. Another reason could be that all of these are individual sports, meaning you have no companions nor any sort of lifeguard to save you in a trouble situation. 

With all that being said it is also important to consider the desires and interests of the people. There are plethora of people who love carrying out such activities. These are the type of people who are constantly in search of adventure and thrill in their lives. They are bored of their daily routine qnd want to experience some twists and turns in life even if it means involving a lot of danger.

I am of the opinion that it is not right to be banning such activities because it is the choice of the individuals as to what they want to do. If they prefer playing such sports involving such high risks then let it be so. Everyone in this world has the right to choose to perform these sports and if it is his or her choice, then it must be respected. 

To conclude with I would like to mention one of my thoughts. Danger can be in the form of anything and everything one does. Even if someone is cycling, running or maybe even just walking then that person could still be in some or the other sort of danger. So, it is not possible to prevent all sorts of dangers instead one should try and enjoy every part of his or her life as long as he or she can. It would most apt to end this essay with an example. A few months back I was at the football stadium viewing an intense match between Argentina and Iran. The match was going on all fine just when one of the players shot the ball really hard which ended up hitting a kid in the stands. I thought to myself,  could viewing a football match turn out to be dangerous as well? I had realised that I had found the answer to my question in the question itself that is anything and everything involves a risk factor,  so all finally depends on your fate. Your fate is what depends whether something turns out to be dangerous to you or not."


Revised:

Here is the edited version of the essay according to the IELTS structure:

"It is argued that extreme sports such as skiing and skydiving are dangerous and should be prohibited. In this essay, I will present and support my position on this matter.

On the one hand, it is true that these activities entail a significant level of risk. One possible reason why these sports are deemed dangerous is the dependence on the equipment used, which is not always entirely reliable and may lead to fatal accidents. Although backup equipment may exist, its effectiveness relies on the user's quick reaction and ability to remain calm in emergency situations. Furthermore, these sports are generally individual pursuits, so no companions or lifeguards are available to assist in dangerous circumstances.

On the other hand, it is important to consider the right of individuals to pursue their interests. Many people seek out these extreme sports to experience the excitement and thrill that they offer, adding a sense of adventure to their otherwise mundane lives.

In my opinion, banning these sports is not the solution. Everyone should have the freedom to decide what constitutes an acceptable risk according to their preferences. As long as the participants are fully aware of the potential dangers and take necessary precautions, they should not be prohibited from taking part in the activities they enjoy.

In conclusion, danger is an inevitable part of life, and no activity is entirely risk-free. Allowing people to engage in extreme sports is a matter of personal choice, and the decision should be respected. To illustrate this point, I recall an incident when a child was hit by a football during a match. This event demonstrates that risk exists in even the most seemingly harmless activities. Ultimately, individuals must assess the level of risk they are willing to take and live life to the fullest."

Lexical Resource:

Từ vựng của bạn khá đa dạng và tương đối chính xác, tuy nhiên, vẫn còn một số lỗi như sau:

1. "It is believed that dangerous activities such as skiing and diving are threatening and should be brought to a stop." - Lỗi: "threatening" không phù hợp với văn phong chính quy trong IELTS, nên sử dụng "dangerous" hoặc "risky" thay cho nó.

2. "Faults in devices used for such sports could even lead on to be fatal. Though backup equipment might be present for the user to make use of in case of an emergency, it needn't be one hundred per cent reliable cause it depends on how quick the user is to react to emergency situations." - "in case of an emergency" là một cụm từ thông thường nhưng không phù hợp cho bài viết IELTS. Thay vào đó, bạn có thể sử dụng từ gợi ý như "in the event of an emergency" để tránh việc lặp lại không cần thiết.

3. "There are plethora of people who love carrying out such activities". - Cụm từ này thiếu "a" khi sử dụng "plethora" như một danh từ đếm được, vì vậy nên viết là "there are a plethora of people".

4. "They are bored of their daily routine qnd want to experience some twists and turns in life even if it means involving a lot of danger." - Từ "qnd" trong câu này có thể là lỗi chính tả. Ngoài ra, bạn nên sử dụng từ khác cho "twists" để tránh lặp lại từ trong đoạn văn.

5. "I had realised that I had found the answer to my question in the question itself that is anything and everything involves a risk factor" - Một cách để cải thiện từ vựng là sử dụng các từ từ khóa đi kèm để biết chắc rằng ý của bạn đang diễn đạt chính xác. Trong câu này, bạn có thể sử dụng từ "hazard" hoặc "peril" thay cho "risk factor".

Với một số cải tiến nhỏ đó, bạn có thể đạt được một bài viết IELTS tốt hơn về từ vựng. Hãy luyện tập thêm trong khi viết để tránh những lỗi trên.
