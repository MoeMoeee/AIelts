"Music has played a pivotal role throughout our history. However, there are mixed opinions about its significance, as many believe it to be a boon for humankind, while others consider it as a curse. Revised:

Here is the edited essay according to IELTS structure:

Music is a part of every society and culture around the world today. While some individuals consider music to bring benefits to individuals and societies, others believe that music has a negative influence. In this essay, both of these views will be discussed before offering a personal opinion on the subject.

Music has played a vital role throughout history. It has transformed society and ignited major revolutions. Music has been a catalyst for almost every social reform. For instance, during the Indian independence movement, the songs penned down by revolutionaries such as Bhagat Singh and Ram Prasad Bismil had a long-lasting impact on the youth, regardless of their cultural belonging. This contributed to raising awareness and eventually helped in achieving independence for India. Furthermore, music has been used as an essential part of medical science to help individuals with their mental health. It is used as a relaxation medium during therapy and also during the treatment of psychological illnesses such as Alzheimer's, anxiety, panic attacks and more.

Despite the benefits of music, it can have a negative impact on society. The success and fame associated with music leads to luxurious lifestyles for musicians and often encourages drug use and alcoholism. As a result, the large followers who aspire to live like these artists engage in similar anti-social activities, leading to a counter-culture attitude among the youth. This can be observed from the activities that took place in the 1960s in the USA as a response to the Vietnam war and Civil rights movement. Musicians largely engaged in drugs and alcohol and inspired a generation of youth to lead a care-free lifestyle, eventually resulting in vandalism and damage to public properties.

In conclusion, while there are negative impacts of music, including the promotion of anti-social behavior in youth, it is my opinion that the positives of music outweigh the negatives and should be promoted worldwide. Music can transform society, ignite revolutions and bring about social reform. Additionally, it can be used as an essential part of medical science for physical and mental health treatment. Therefore, music plays a crucial role in our society and culture.
