Topic:

"Thanks to developments in technology, many people today have lost basic skills such as how to repair clothes, etc. Why is this happening? Do you think that this is a negative development?"

Essay:

"Technology has transformed our world in all aspects. While it has brought forward the biggest revolution in the form of the internet, on the other hand, it has provided us with different machines that have made many of our day-to-day tasks easy. However, there is a general perception that it has made people forget about some basic life skills . In  the following paragraphs, I would like to shed some light on the bases of such thoughts.

First of all, as we all know  technology has made our lives easier in many ways and humans have worked hard for years to acquire this all. For example, we have now automated washing machines and dishwasher present in our homes that can do the work in minutes for us without any hassle. So. basically in my view, it is only about utilising the best facilities and it is not in any way related to laziness or lack of skills. Individuals are just thriving to save their time , so they can invest it in the  better side but a lot of personages from old generation regarded it as a lack of interest in life skills.

Furthermore, the internet has opened many new doors for learning and mastering new techniques of all types. For instance, girls can cook from easy cooking videos and teachers can get ideas for many artworks. Students can take online lectures and online workshops that can help them in mastering many skills.  Therefore, instead of making individuals lazy and raw, it is actually polishing them in the long run

To sum up, it can be concluded with full confidence that advancement has literally brought the best for us. Now, it is our responsibility to use it for a positive impact and avoid any misuse. We should also try to learn not to be always dependent on anything but we should make the most of the facilities we have now."


Revised:

Technology has revolutionized various aspects of our lives, from the internet to machines that simplify our daily tasks. However, there is a concern that these advancements have led people to neglect basic life skills, such as repairing clothes. In this essay, I will discuss the reasons behind this phenomenon and present my opinion on whether it is a negative development.

Firstly, it is undeniable that technology has made our lives more convenient. Take washing machines and dishwashers as examples - they allow us to perform these chores quickly and effortlessly. Consequently, some argue that relying on such machines does not reflect laziness or a lack of skills, but rather a desire to save time for other productive activities. However, this has led older generations to perceive it as a diminishing interest in essential life skills.

Secondly, the internet has provided limitless opportunities for learning and mastering new techniques. Through online videos, individuals can access easy cooking recipes, teachers can find inspiration for creative projects, and students can attend virtual lectures and workshops to acquire various skills. Instead of making individuals lazy or ignorant, the internet serves as a platform for personal growth and development in the long run.

In conclusion, technological advancements have undoubtedly brought numerous advantages to society. It is our responsibility to utilize these advancements positively, avoiding any misuse. While it is important to not solely depend on technology, we should make the most of the facilities available to us. With the proper balance between technology and traditional life skills, we can continue to progress and thrive.
