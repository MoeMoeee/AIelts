Revised:

Nowadays, many companies have developed computer systems and created working robots for multiple purposes. Some people believe that this technology may help them to work effectively, while others argue that there are many negative effects of robots taking over jobs from humans. This essay will explain the benefits and drawbacks of this development and argue that this technology should not completely replace human jobs.

To begin with, artificial intelligence programs can support many companies, such as food industries and hardware manufacturers. Programmed robots can work faster than humans and at a constant rate, leading to increased productivity for companies. Moreover, robots can maintain product quality. For example, the food industry has been using robots to prepare their frozen products, ensuring consistent cutting sizes and processing speeds. Although this technology can help many industries, there are still tasks that cannot be effectively replaced by machines.

However, while robots have their advantages in various fields, they have limitations in performing creative works, critical thinking skills, and hands-on procedures. Although some companies attempt to use robots to create artwork, the results have not been satisfactory. The style of art produced by robots often lack creativity as they simply copy from other artists. Furthermore, robots are being explored for medical applications, including assisting doctors in diagnosing diseases. However, they still lack the accuracy to understand complicated medical conditions. Many practical skills are required for specialists to effectively perform these tasks.

In conclusion, while the use of robots can be beneficial for mass production and standard manufacturing, they cannot completely replace humans in creative works and medical fields. This essay argues that there are more disadvantages to this development. While robots can certainly enhance efficiency and productivity, humans still possess unique skills and abilities that are essential for tasks that require creativity, critical thinking, and complex problem-solving. Therefore, it is important to harmonize the use of robots with the presence of human workers to achieve the best outcomes.
