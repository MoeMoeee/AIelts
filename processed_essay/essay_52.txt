Topic:

"At present the population of some countries include a relatively large number of young adults, compared with the number of older people.
Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages? Give reasons for your answer with relevant example."

Essay:

"In recent times, some nations have a comparatively large number of young people than the number of older people. Possessing younger people than  older ones has both positive and negative sides as well. However,  positivity never outweighs the negativity. The following essay will explore the two sides and provide a plausible example. 

To start with, there is a saying that young adults are the future of any nation. The contribution of young adults in numerous sectors of the country is huge. Hence, with the help of their strength and ,fitness they easily take the leadership of any sector. For example, the economy, employment, sports and defence cannot be imagined without the performance of young people. Moreover, young people have the courage and motivation to take responsibility which cannot be done by  older people.  For example, a recent study demonstrates that, young people have 60% more stamina and courage than old people. Therefore, countries are fully dependent on the young generation more than the old ones.

Conversely, if the volume of the young generation outweighs the old ones obviously that will have some demerits. Building a nation with both younger and older people is necessary. The old people are the forerunners of  a country and the young generation always follow their footprints. For example, the old generation can easily be friends, philosophers and guides of the young generation. Furthermore, the old generation can help the young generation in learning new things and ideas and share their experiences with the younger generation. Therefore, to build a country both young and old people are required.

To conclude, although the volume of the young generation outweighs the old generation, it does not outweigh the merits of the younger generation. However, to achieve a balanced society and nation there are no alternatives for younger people.
"


Revised:

Here is the edited version of the essay with IELTS structure:

Introduction:
In some countries, there is a higher proportion of young adults than older people. While this has advantages and disadvantages, this essay will argue that the drawbacks of an imbalanced age population outweigh the benefits.

Body Paragraph 1:
Having a large number of young adults is beneficial for a country in several ways. Firstly, young people are often seen as the future of a nation, and they can contribute greatly to various sectors such as the economy, employment, sports, and defense. They possess the strength, fitness, and motivation necessary to take on leadership roles, which older people may not have. For example, recent studies have shown that young people have 60% more stamina and courage than older people.

Body Paragraph 2:
Despite its advantages, a skewed age population can have negative effects on a country. Older people play a crucial role in a society and make significant contributions. They serve as mentors and guides to young people and can share their experiences and knowledge. Hence, it is essential to have a balance of both older and younger individuals to create a well-rounded society.

Conclusion:
In conclusion, while the positives of having more young people may seem appealing, the downsides of an imbalanced age population are not to be ignored. To achieve a prosperous society, both older and younger people must work together to create a harmonious nation. Therefore, it is crucial to maintain a balance between the two age groups and recognize the contributions of both young and old individuals.

1. "Possessing younger people"
Từ "possessing" không phù hợp trong trường hợp này, bạn nên sử dụng "having" hoặc "having a greater number of" thay cho "possessing".

2. "strength and, fitness they easily take the leadership"
Câu này cần phải sửa lỗi trình tự từ, bạn cần phải thêm một từ để kết nối "strength and" với "fitness" trong câu này, nhưng nếu không có từ này thì cũng không ảnh hưởng nhiều đến ý nghĩa của câu.

Câu này cần sử dụng một từ nối phù hợp để kết nối "employment, sports, and defense" với "the economy", ví dụ như "as" hoặc "since".

Thiếu từ "then" sau từ "ones", và nên sử dụng một cấu trúc khác biệt, ví dụ như "the disadvantages of this situation would then become apparent".

6. "Building a nation with both younger and older people is necessary."
Sau "necessary" cần phải điền từ "in", để câu trở nên đầy đủ và chính xác.
