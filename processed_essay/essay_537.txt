Here is the edited version of the essay, taking into consideration the IELTS structure:

"It is true that historical sites and museums predominantly attract tourists rather than local people. This can be attributed to several reasons. However, there are various measures that can be taken to encourage locals to visit these historical sites and museums. This essay will explore the reasons behind this trend and propose potential solutions.

One reason why local people are less interested in visiting historical sites and museums is due to their familiarity with their own cities. Local citizens often feel that they already have sufficient knowledge about their city's history, making such visits redundant and unexciting. On the other hand, tourists are enthusiastic about exploring new places and learning about the history and culture of the countries they visit. Additionally, the high entrance fees of these establishments act as a deterrent for local people. Once they have visited a historical site or museum, they may be reluctant to pay the high entrance fees again.

To address this issue, several solutions can be implemented. Firstly, the government should organize campaigns aimed at attracting the local public to these establishments. This can be done through the provision of free educational seminars, health care camps, and other programs that are of interest to the local community. Moreover, authorities responsible for these historical sites and museums should offer extra discounts or even free entry to locals. This would make it more affordable for low- and middle-income families to visit and appreciate their cultural heritage. For instance, educational institutions can assign history-related projects to students, encouraging them to have direct interactions with these historical places and fostering a deeper understanding of their significance. Lastly, organizations managing these sites can also provide increased job opportunities for local residents. This would not only encourage them to work in these establishments but also allow them to develop a greater appreciation for history.

In conclusion, in order to attract more local visitors to historical sites and museums, the government and authorities should implement measures such as organizing exhibits, offering free or subsidized entry, and providing job opportunities. By taking these steps, the local public's interest in visiting these establishments can be significantly increased."
