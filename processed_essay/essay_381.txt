Topic:

"You are working for a company. You need to take some time off work and want to ask your manager about this.

Write a letter to your manager. In your letter:

explain why you want to take time off work

give details of the amount of time you need

suggest how your work could be covered while you are away"

Essay:

"Dear Sir

My name is Nam Nguyen and I am the emploee of Happy Dragon Technology Company. I am very happy to work under your intructions and I have been working here for over ten years.

 I am writing this letter to ask your help because my son had very illness and right now he is in the hospital. My wife do not have time to take care of him the whole day. That is why she ask me to take some times off work go to the hospital to take care of him. Can I ask two hours off in the afternoon, from 5pm until 7pm. I want to use this time to be with my son and take care of him. Instead of the quality of my work less, I will spend more energy to finish my job before 5 pm.

 I hope everything all right and my son can recover soon so that I can contiune to support the company.

Best regrading

Nam Nguyen"


Revised:

Dear Sir,

I am writing to request some time off work from Happy Dragon Technology Company. I have recently received news that my son is ill and is currently admitted in the hospital. As my wife is unable to take care of him throughout the day, she has requested that I take some time off to be with him. Therefore, I would like to ask for a two-hour break in the afternoon from 5 pm until 7 pm.

During this time period, I intend to be by my son's side and attend to his needs. However, I assure you that this will not affect the quality of my work. In fact, I will make extra efforts to complete my tasks before 5 pm to ensure that my responsibilities are fulfilled promptly. I understand the importance of my role and I will make sure any pending work is either completed or delegated to a colleague who can cover for me during my absence.

I sincerely hope that you would grant me this time off so that I can fulfill my responsibilities as a parent and support my family during this challenging time. Your understanding and support in this matter would be greatly appreciated.

Thank you for your attention to this request. I will eagerly await your response and do my best to ensure a smooth transition during my absence.

Best regards,

Nam Nguyen
