Topic:
"We are becoming increasingly dependent on computer-based technology. How do you think it will change in the future? Is it good for us to rely so much on computers?"

Essay:

The increasing dependence on computer-based technology has become a controversial topic globally. This essay aims to discuss the future prospects of technology and propose its advantages. From my perspective, people will become more dependent on technology, and this phenomenon is beneficial. 

To begin with, technological advancements have the potential to revolutionize both social and professional aspects of people's lives. The development of technology has made it more convenient than in previous years to do every task, from communicating over long distances through smartphones or social networking platforms such as Facebook or Instagram to reducing the burden of household chores for most women. Technology has also contributed to a more efficient means of meal preparation by allowing individuals to select a meal by choosing from the screen of their fridge or stove instead of cooking from scratch. Furthermore, it has allowed most people to work and learn remotely, reducing the need for physical offices and classrooms, thereby reducing expenses.

While some people argue that excessive reliance on computers can have negative consequences, it is essential to consider the advantages that technology has brought to human life. Firstly, machinery can save time for individuals and provide them with opportunities to live a healthier lifestyle. Thanks to some household appliances such as washing machines or dishwashers, people can leave the housework for them and spend that time engaging in physical activities, ultimately improving their health. Secondly, technology has significantly improved people's living standards. In the past, people had to communicate in-person or through letters, but with the internet, they can have virtual conversations, which is much more convenient, especially during the pandemic. Besides, they can entertain themselves with electronic appliances such as television or video games, which are great innovations in the revolution of technology.

In conclusion, computers and technology have become essential in modern-day living, and people will continue to rely on them in the future. In my opinion, the advancements in technology have propelled innovation, made human life more comfortable, efficient, and entertaining. Thus, it is safe to say that the benefits of technology outweigh its negative impacts.
