Revised:

In recent times, individuals are increasingly required to work longer hours, which has led to heightened levels of stress when compared to previous generations. There are several reasons behind this trend, such as increased job demands and the availability of technology. However, there are measures that employers can implement to improve the work-life balance of their employees. These include reducing the number of work hours and refraining from contacting employees outside of official working hours.

On one hand, one of the main reasons people are working longer hours nowadays is due to heightened job demands. Companies often demand that their employees work longer hours in order to increase productivity. This is particularly evident in today's competitive job market, where companies strive to provide superior services to their customers. Furthermore, the availability of technology has also contributed to extended work hours. Employers can easily contact their workers through various means, such as computers and smartphones, which can put excessive pressure on employees. For example, some employers may contact their workers during late hours or infringe on their family time.

However, on the other hand, employers can take certain measures to mitigate these issues. One effective solution is for employers to implement policies that reduce work hours. For example, in Saudi Arabia, all employees are subject to a maximum of 48 working hours per week. Furthermore, employers should refrain from contacting their employees outside of official working hours. This will provide employees with the necessary space and time to relax and spend quality time with their families.

In conclusion, there are various reasons why individuals are frequently required to work longer hours in recent times, including increased job demands and the availability of technology. However, employers can play a crucial role in improving the work-life balance of their employees by reducing work hours and refraining from contacting them outside of official working hours. By implementing these measures, employers can help alleviate stress and promote a healthier work environment for their workers.
