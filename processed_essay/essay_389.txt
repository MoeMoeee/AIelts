Revised:

In academic life, it is increasingly encouraged to act as a positive role model within the school in order to portray an admirable and amiable figure. This movement allows students to have the best learning experiences and gain vital knowledge for their personal growth. This report will discuss the advantages and disadvantages of being a positive role model in school.

To begin with, being a good role model encourages students to look up to individuals who possess exceptional qualities such as confidence, assertiveness, leadership, and social skills. This provides them with valuable life lessons and helps educate children about moral behavior. By observing and learning from positive role models, students can develop into responsible, respectful adults who are willing to make significant contributions to society. For example, over their 12 years at school, students interact with various individuals who shape their personalities. If they find someone who aligns with their values and beliefs, they have the potential to become individuals with incredible capabilities to lead others to tremendous achievements.

On the other hand, having negative role models can lead young students down a scandal-ridden path. Negative role models can provide terrible experiences and negatively impact the development of useful personality traits. Behaviors such as laziness, disrespect, and aggressiveness can lead children towards engaging in illegal activities. Therefore, being a positive role model not only protects young students from an unwanted future but also provides an opportunity for them to develop their full potential, inspire others, and achieve personal growth.

In conclusion, acting as a positive role model is crucial and beneficial, not only for the individuals themselves but also for society as a whole. This process allows children to observe positive behaviors and be positively reinforced for their own learning and development. It is important for schools and educators to promote positive role models to ensure the best learning environment for students.
