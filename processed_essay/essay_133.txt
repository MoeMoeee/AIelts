Revised:

It is argued that the health of individuals will decline in the future compared to the present. I strongly agree with this notion. 

On the one hand, people in contemporary times are often preoccupied with their work and family commitments. Their professional occupation frequently induces stress, which they find difficult to manage. Although individuals work hard, dedicating long hours and sometimes overtime, to pursue financial stability, they fail to allocate sufficient time for exercises such as yoga, sports, or gym activities. Additionally, many families resort to consuming fast-food or street food, as it’s a convenient option. However, such foods are unhealthy and contain high levels of calories, increasing the risk of heart disease, diabetes, and obesity. 

On the other hand, the environmental conditions are deteriorating at an alarming rate, ultimately impacting the health of individuals. Firstly, the deforestation rate is accelerating, leading to an increase in carbon dioxide levels, which leads to global warming. Secondly, the unchecked disposal of waste from factories has polluted the air and water sources, resulting in severe respiratory illnesses among individuals. Furthermore, the increase in the number of vehicles has contributed to high levels of air pollution in cities, leading to significant health implications.

To conclude, although individuals have become increasingly aware of their health, numerous factors continue to pose a threat to the average standard of people's health. People should prioritize lifestyle changes to ensure a healthy and prosperous future. They must reduce their stress levels, engage in regular exercise, adopt a healthy diet, and actively participate in improving the environment by promoting sustainable development practices.

1. "It is argued that the people's health is more and more lower than in the future compared with people's health nowadays. l totally agree with this statement."

Ở đoạn này, bạn sử dụng "more and more lower" nhưng từ "more" đã bao gồm nghĩa "hơn", do vậy không cần dùng từ "more" hai lần. Bạn có thể sửa thành "will be lower in the future than it is now" hoặc "is likely to deteriorate in the future compared to the current state of people's health". 

2. "Firstly, they sometime feel stress with their work but they can not live that they don't work."

Trong câu này, bạn đã dùng sai thời của động từ "live". Thay vì dùng "live", bạn nên sử dụng "survive" để sử dụng đúng nghĩa của câu. Ngoài ra, từ "sometime" nên được sửa thành "sometimes" để đúng chính tả.

Ở đây, từ "such as" được sử dụng sai. "Such as" chỉ được sử dụng để liệt kê và chỉ định các ví dụ cụ thể. Do vậy, bạn cần thay thế "such as" bằng "like" để sử dụng đúng trường hợp.
