Topic:

"Your English-speaking friend who lives in your town has asked for your advice about learning a new sport.

Write an email to your friend.

Recommend a new sport that would be suitable for your friend to learn

Explain how your fiend could learn this sport

Suggest that you both learn this sport together."

Essay:

"Dear Jahid,

 I hope you are doing well. I am so happy to receive a letter from you last week. Today, I am writing this letter to give you a suggestion for learning a new sport which is basketball.

 Basketball is a very fun sport. To learn to play this sport, we need to follow a few steps. The first step is building stamina. Building stamina is an essential goal for this sport. To achieve this goal, we can take 10 laps in the whole street. After accomplishing our first goal, we can start dribbling around the whole court for about 20 minutes. Then, we can take some shots around the whole court until we get better. After we have accomplished all three goals, we can start playing a 1 on 1 match to get easier with the basic rules. Therefore, practising this game with each other can be beneficial for both of us to stay fit and learn a new game. I am sure you will absolutely like this sport. I am hoping to hear back from you soon. Hope we can start by next week. Please let me know your convenient time after work. 

Best Regards,

 Nazmul"


Revised:

Dear Jahid,

I hope this email finds you well. I was delighted to receive your letter last week and I am writing to suggest a new sport for you to learn: basketball.

Basketball is not only an enjoyable sport, but it also offers numerous benefits. To begin learning this sport, it is important to follow a systematic approach. Firstly, it is crucial to build stamina, which is essential for basketball. We can achieve this by jogging 10 laps around the court or the local park. Once our stamina has improved, we can move on to dribbling the ball around the court for approximately 20 minutes. This will help us develop better ball control. Additionally, we can practice shooting from various positions on the court until our accuracy improves. Lastly, we can engage in friendly one-on-one matches to familiarize ourselves with the basic rules of the game.

Learning basketball together would be advantageous for both of us. Not only will it help us stay fit and active, but it will also provide an opportunity for friendly competition and camaraderie. I am confident that you will enjoy this sport as much as I do.

I suggest we begin our basketball training as soon as possible. Please let me know your availability after work or over the weekend so that we can schedule our practice sessions accordingly. I am eager to hear back from you and excited to embark on this new sporting journey together.

Looking forward to your response.

Best regards,
Nazmul
