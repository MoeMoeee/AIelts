Parenting and dealing with teenagers can be challenging tasks in the modern era. In this essay, I will discuss both viewpoints and share my opinion on the matter.

On one hand, there are valid reasons why parents should monitor their children during their teenage years. Firstly, adolescents have limited life experience compared to their parents. Secondly, parents have the right to guide and support their children so they can become successful individuals. Many teenagers nowadays prioritize social media over education, which can leave them without the necessary skills or qualifications to compete in the job market later in life.

On the other hand, proponents argue that parents should give their children the freedom to make decisions based on their preferences. Furthermore, conflicts between parents and teenagers only lead to strained relationships within the family. It is therefore suggested that families should avoid conflicts and instead spend quality time together.

In my opinion, conflicts between parents and teenagers only create misunderstandings and tension. Therefore, it is best for decisions to be made through collaborative efforts between both parties. This approach can help prevent unnecessary stress and foster a harmonious relationship.

In conclusion, there are differing opinions regarding conflicts between parents and young adults during the teenage years. However, I believe that conflicts are ultimately fruitless and a waste of time.
