Write a letter to console your friend. In your letter:

Express your sympathy

Invite your friend to do something with you."

Essay:

"Dear Jun,

How are you? I hope you're doing well. I writing this letter to tell you that I just heard the news about your dog had passed away. I'm sorry to hear that because I know he'd been accompany you for long time. 

We created lots of memories together, especially with your dog. In addition. I remember the first time you introduced your pet, you feel so delighted and took a lot of pictures. Moreover, your dog always love to licks someone's hand when he feels comfortable with that person, to show his kindness. 

Considering you current situations, I would like to invite you to go to the beach, as far as I know, when you're sad, you always seek the sea to make yourself feel at ease. Even though, it's hard to accept this situation. Furthermore, how about if I baked some of your favorite biscuits to make you smile? I just don't want you to feel sad all the time. 

I am looking forward to your reply soon.

Warm regards,

Jun"

Dear Jun,

Given the difficult time you are going through, I would like to extend an invitation to you. How about we plan a trip to the beach? I know that being near the sea has always brought you a sense of peace and tranquility. It might be helpful for you to find solace in nature and take some time to heal. Additionally, I would be more than happy to bake some of your favorite biscuits. The aroma and taste of these treats might bring a smile to your face amidst these trying times. 

Please know that I am here for you, Jun. I want to be a source of support and comfort for you during this period of grief. You don't have to go through this alone.

I am eagerly awaiting your response.

Warm regards,

Your Friend
