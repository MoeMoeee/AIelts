"Some people think that it is fine for professional sportmen and sportwomen to misbehave on or off the field, as long as they are playing well.

Do you agree or disagree this statement?"

Essay:

"It is argued that the best way to solve the problems related with environment is to increase the cost of fuel. I completely disagree with this idea for several distinct reasons.

On the one hand, it may be right solution for environment protection to increase the cost of fuel as nowadays everyone has his or her private car. Naturally, the rate of fuel usage is very high and it tend to pollute the environment . Moreover, when fossil fuels are burned the release large amounts of carbon dioxide and greenhouse gas into the air. Greenhouse gases trap heat in our atmosphere causing global warming. Because of these problems the cost of fossil fuel should be increased to reduce problems with environment.

However, I do not believe that it may reduce all environmental problems as there are other things that impact on environment more than fuels. First of all, human activity is causing environmental degradation, which is the deterioration of the environment through depletion of resources  such as air, water, soil and destruction  of ecosystem and and the extinction of wildlife. For instance, when waters run dry people cannot get enough to drink, wash or feed crops and economic  decline may occur. In addition inadequate sanitation a problem for 2.4 billion people can lead to deadly diarrheal diseases, including  cholera and thyphoid fever  and other water-borne illnesses.

In conclusion, I think increasing costs of fuels could not solve all environmental problems as there are many factors that influence on environment."

There is a debate on whether professional athletes should be allowed to misbehave on or off the field as long as they perform well. In my opinion, this idea is unacceptable for various reasons.

On the one hand, some argue that athletes who excel in their sport should be given more leeway in their conduct. They may argue that entertainment or competition is the primary goal of a sport, and that as long as the players deliver results, their personal behavior should not be scrutinized too rigorously. However, I disagree with this viewpoint. Firstly, athletes, like everyone else, should abide by fundamental ethical principles such as respect, fairness, and decency. If a player engages in violence, cheating, or hateful language, it not only reflects poorly on their character but also sets a bad example for fans, especially young ones. Moreover, even if the player's skills are exceptional, their misconduct may cost their team or even their country a victory, or cause injuries to themselves or others. Therefore, sports authorities should enforce strict codes of conduct and penalize those who violate them, regardless of their performance level.

On the other hand, some may argue that athletes' off-field behavior is their private matter and should not be subject to public scrutiny. They may argue that as long as the players fulfill their contractual obligations and do not break any laws, their personal lives should be immune from criticism or exposure. However, I also disagree with this viewpoint. Firstly, professional athletes are public figures who receive significant attention and influence from the media and fans. Their actions off the field can affect their image, sponsors, and even their team's reputation. If a player is caught using drugs, driving drunk, or assaulting someone, it can damage their career and hurt the sports community. Moreover, athletes should be role models who promote positive values and behaviors, not simply skilled performers who entertain the crowd. Therefore, sports organizations should require their athletes to behave responsibly and set high standards for their conduct.

In conclusion, I believe that professional athletes should not be allowed to misbehave on or off the field, regardless of their performance level. Athletes should be accountable for their actions and demonstrate integrity, sportsmanship, and civility. Sports can serve as a platform for promoting positive social values and inspiring people to strive for excellence, and athletes have a responsibility to use their talent and fame for good."

Chính vì thế, để sử dụng ngữ cảnh chính xác, bạn có thể thay thế "environment" bằng một số từ khác như "ecosystem", "habitats", "natural resources",...

Grammatical Range and Accuracy:

Bài viết của bạn cũng có một số lỗi ngữ pháp. Sau đây là một số lỗi mà tôi đã tìm thấy và gợi ý cho bạn cách sửa chúng:

1. "It is argued that the best way to solve the problems related with environment is to increase the cost of fuel." 

Sửa thành: "It is argued that the best way to solve problems related to the environment is to increase the cost of fuel."

Lời giải thích: Không cần "with" ở đây, chỉ cần "related to" là đủ.

2. "Naturally, the rate of fuel usage is very high and it tend to pollute the environment."

Sửa thành: "Naturally, the rate of fuel usage is very high and it tends to pollute the environment."

Lời giải thích: "Tend" là động từ số lưỡng nên cần phải đổi thành "tends".

3. "Moreover, when fossil fuels are burned the release large amounts of carbon dioxide and greenhouse gas into the air."

Sửa thành: "Moreover, when fossil fuels are burned, they release large amounts of carbon dioxide and greenhouse gases into the air."

Lời giải thích: Thiếu một động từ ở đây và cần phải thêm một số ít lỗi chính tả, chia động từ và đa dạng ngữ pháp.

- "resources  such as air" -> "resources such as air"
- "and and the extinction of wildlife." -> "and the extinction of wildlife."

Tổng thể, tuy vẫn còn một số lỗi, tuy nhiên việc sử dụng ngữ pháp của bạn là tương đối chính xác và linh hoạt. Đối với việc viết bài thi IELTS, cần chú ý đến sự chính xác, đa dạng và sử dụng ngữ pháp phù hợp để tăng điểm cho tiêu chí này.
