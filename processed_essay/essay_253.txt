Revised:

Nowadays, there is an increasing trend of traveling across countries, especially during the holiday season. People plan trips to various countries such as Switzerland, China, the USA, and Canada to explore the Western side. While traveling as a tourist, individuals often desire to experience different paths in the country they are visiting.

When someone travels as a tourist for a few weeks or months, their main aim is to enjoy their time there. However, some believe that there is more to gain from their travel experience. It is of utmost importance to learn about certain aspects of the country, such as their religion, social values, weather conditions, and business practices.

I strongly agree that spending only a few days in a hotel as a tourist is not sufficient to truly understand and appreciate a country. Living in a hotel restricts one's ability to fully immerse themselves in the local culture. Additionally, prices tend to be higher during the tourist season, which can limit the resources available to explore all areas of the country. Therefore, if one wishes to truly understand and appreciate a country, they should consider living there for a longer period of time to gain more specific and accurate knowledge.

Moreover, each region of the world has its own unique specialties. For example, in Pakistan, there are various products available such as the food in Karachi, traditional clothing in Hunza, traditional games, and famous tourist spots like Murree and Swat. To gain a comprehensive understanding of these aspects, one must spend a reasonable amount of time living in the country.

In conclusion, while tourism is encouraged worldwide and can boost a country's economy, it is essential to go beyond superficial enjoyment and seek a deeper understanding of the country being visited. This requires living in the country for a longer duration to fully appreciate its culture, traditions, and specialties.
