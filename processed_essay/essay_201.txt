Topic:

"SOME PEOPLE BELIEVE THAT EVENTUALLY ALL JOBS WILL BE DONE BY ARTIFICIALLY INTELLIGENT ROBOTS. WHAT IS YOUR OPINION?"

Essay:

"These days with the advent of new technologies such as AI, a lot of people have lost their businesses and have been replaced by them. Someone may say that this process will continue, resulting in the loose of all the jobs. While others say that there are professions that only living humans can do. I agree with the latter opinion and process this view in this essay.
Most importantly, professions dealing with feelings are difficult to be managed by AI. It is a privilege for human beings to be moved, shocked or angry and needless to say, those emotions can not be understood by artificially made things. For example, jobs such as mental counsellors can not be replaced. This is because there are no patterns or concrete solutions that can be applied to a mental problem. Problems regarding sentiments are always different and so the solutions are uncountable like stars. It is impossible for AI to deal with it using a pattern programmed previously. 
On the other hand, someone may say that if we give all the accumulated information about a job, AI will be able to run the work. I agree to some extent but the more jobs AI steals, the more works people will nurture. AI is special in that it can use effectively all the past information. However, it is unable to make new things because it lacks creativity. Therefore, only humans are able to make innovations and innovative jobs can not be replaced by humans. 
In conclusion, AI will never replace all jobs because it can not understand feelings and give birth to innovations.
"


Revised:

Revised essay:

Nowadays, with the introduction of advanced technologies such as Artificial Intelligence (AI), people have lost their jobs and been replaced by machines. Some argue that this trend will continue, and eventually all jobs will be performed by AI. However, I disagree with this view as certain professions require human involvement. I will elaborate on this viewpoint in the following essay.

Firstly, jobs that deal with emotions cannot be performed by AI. Humans are unique in experiencing emotions such as joy, anger, and sadness. These feelings cannot be understood by machines. For instance, jobs such as mental counselors and therapists cannot be replaced by technology. These jobs require a personal touch, where the solutions to each problem are tailored to the individual's unique needs and circumstances. As a result, it is impossible for AI to handle such jobs using pre-programmed patterns.

On the other hand, some may argue that by providing AI with all the relevant information, it can perform the job better. Although I partially agree with this claim, as AI can perform specific tasks that are repetitive and require the processing of large amounts of data, it is not capable of innovation. AI can effectively use past information but cannot create anything new since it lacks human creativity. Only humans can invent new and innovative solutions to problems, and these jobs are irreplaceable.

In conclusion, AI will never replace all jobs due to its inability to understand human emotions and innovate. While it is true that AI can perform certain jobs more efficiently than humans, there are critical positions that can only be filled by people who possess human touch and creativity. Therefore, it is safe to say that AI will be supplementing human efforts in the future rather than completely replacing us.
