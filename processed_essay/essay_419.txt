Essay:

Some people argue that violence portrayed on television and in computer games has a harmful effect on society, while others refute the claim, stating that these factors do not significantly influence people’s behavior. In my opinion, violence in media does have a detrimental impact on society and individuals.

In today's society, the use of video games on various types of consoles is escalating at an alarming rate. Children are deeply engrossed in these modern forms of entertainment, particularly games that revolve around violence and combat. The effects of these games on society are dangerously negative. However, there are some who believe that these games have little to no effect on an individual's behavior. In the following paragraphs, I will elaborate on the reasons why I believe these types of games can negatively affect children's lives in our society.

Firstly, proponents of violent games argue that children engage with games in different ways. Some children prefer to play games on computers and mobile phones, while others opt for the latest consoles. These modern games often feature fighting themes, with players attacking and fighting against each other. For instance, the game PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) consists of creating a battlefield where 100 players are dropped, divided into groups of four. They fight against each other using various weapons. Tragically, in 2020, a crime was reported in Lahore, Pakistan, where a boy killed his mother and sister with a gun, reminiscent of the game's theme. Subsequent investigations revealed that the boy had spent countless hours playing PUBG and was mentally unstable. A similar incident occurred in New Zealand, where a man killed several people in a mosque.

On the other hand, there are those who argue that violent video games do not have any negative impact on individuals. They contend that these games promote a sense of command and decision-making skills in children.

In conclusion, the negative effects of violent video games outweigh any potential benefits. Numerous incidents have been directly linked to these types of games, which demonstrate their detrimental influence on individuals. However, it is important to note that there may be some minimal advantages, such as improved decision-making abilities in children. Nonetheless, it is crucial for society to acknowledge and address the harmful effects of violence portrayed in television and computer games.
