In addition to their environmental benefits, electric cars offer substantial cost savings. Primarily, the operational costs of electric cars are considerably lower compared to their petrol counterparts. This is mainly due to the lower cost of electricity as a fuel source in comparison to petrol. Furthermore, the maintenance expenses for electric cars are significantly lower, as they require less frequent servicing and have fewer components that are prone to wear and tear. Additionally, the option of installing a solar charger at home eliminates the need to rely on external charging stations, providing even more convenience and cost savings. Many governments worldwide provide incentives and grants to promote the adoption of electric cars, further facilitating cost-effective transportation choices.

In conclusion, banning the use of petrol and diesel cars in favor of electric vehicles can yield numerous benefits for both the environment and individuals. Electric cars offer a sustainable solution that mitigates air and noise pollution, as well as reducing greenhouse gas emissions.
