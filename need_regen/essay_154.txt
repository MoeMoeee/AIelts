Topic:

"Some people think that professional athletes make good role models for young people, while others believe they don’t.

Discuss both these points of views and give your own opinion."

Essay:

"Every famous person's activity has an effect somewhere on other people. Some people say that professional players make good role models for the younger generation, while others think that this is not right. Through this essay I will discuss both of the points in detail and then will come to a reasonable conclusion comprising of my opinion.

Famous players can play a vital role in the youth development. They are followed by many people, because of their reputation and hence their every action has a direct effect on their followers. For instance, the increasing change in fashion and the lifestyle of the youth, is caused by the specialist sports person because of their renowned status. Thus, it this clear that how important role they can play in order to alter the attitude of people.

On the other hand, there are people, who argue that this saying is not right, because they believe that every person is capable enough to make the difference between right and wrong. It is further added that, though, such distinguished players have the reputation and respect in society, but this does not mean that they must be followed in every moment of life. However, this is not always true, because it is difficult to control the youngster from adopting their style as a fan can do anything for his / her followers. Thus, this thought is refused because of such reason.

In conclusion, it is clear that professional sports persons can play a vital role in order to affect the lives of youth positively, because they have fans and followers everywhere. Thus, I believe that it is right to say that esteemed sports person can make a good role model for the younger generation of a community."

Revised:

It is often debated whether professional athletes are good role models for young people. While some argue that their fame and status make them influential figures, others believe that individuals should be able to distinguish right from wrong without external influence. In this essay, both perspectives will be examined before presenting a personal opinion.

On one hand, professional athletes can have a positive impact on the development of young people. Due to their celebrity status, they have a significant influence on the behavior and attitudes of their followers. For example, they can inspire individuals to pursue active and healthy lifestyles or even instill values such as hard work and determination. Therefore, it is clear that sports figures can play a crucial role in shaping the character of young people.

On the other hand, there are those who contend that individuals should not rely on celebrity figures as role models. They argue that every person has the capacity to differentiate between right and wrong and that children should be taught this skill. Moreover, they believe that blindly following the behavior of athletes can result in poor decision-making and negative outcomes. However, this argument fails to acknowledge the significant influence that athletes can have on impressionable young people.

In conclusion, it is evident that professional athletes possess the ability to shape the values and behaviors of young people. While it is important to ensure that children learn to distinguish between right and wrong independently, it cannot be denied that athletes have a significant impact on the impressionable youth. Therefore, it can be concluded that professional athletes can serve as positive role models for younger generations.

Coherence and Cohesion:?

Overall, your essay demonstrates decent coherence and cohesion in terms of logical organization and connection between ideas. However, there are a few areas where the cohesion could be improved:

1. Introduction: The introduction could be more explicit and engaging. Instead of simply stating that every famous person's activity has an effect on others, consider providing a more specific and captivating opening statement or anecdote related to professional athletes as role models.

2. Topic sentences: Each body paragraph should start with a clear and specific topic sentence that introduces the main idea of the paragraph. In your essay, the topic sentences are generally clear, but they lack the necessary specificity. For example, in the second body paragraph, the topic sentence could be revised to explicitly state how professional athletes can play a role in altering attitudes and changing the lifestyle of youth.

3. Development of arguments: While you present arguments in both body paragraphs, they could be further developed and supported with specific examples, evidence, or personal experiences. This would enhance the coherence and persuasiveness of your essay. For instance, in the second body paragraph, you can provide concrete examples of how professional athletes have influenced fashion and lifestyle choices among young people.

4. Transitions: Although your essay contains some transition words (e.g., "thus," "on the other hand"), there is room for improvement. Try to incorporate more varied and precise transition words/phrases to guide the reader smoothly from one idea to another. For example, instead of using "thus" repeatedly, consider using other phrases such as "consequently," "as a result," or "therefore."

5. Conclusion: The conclusion effectively summarizes your main points, but it could be strengthened by briefly addressing the opposing viewpoint and presenting a balanced perspective before restating your own opinion.

To improve the coherence and cohesion of your essay, you should focus on providing more specific examples, strengthening your topic sentences, using varied transition words, and considering a more engaging introduction and well-rounded conclusion.


Lexical Resource:?

Your essay demonstrates a fairly good command of vocabulary and a reasonable use of contextual language. However, there are a few areas where improvements can be made. Let's go through them in detail:

1. "Famous players can play a vital role in the youth development." 
   The phrase "youth development" is better replaced with "the development of young people" or "the growth of the younger generation".

2. "They are followed by many people, because of their reputation and hence their every action has a direct effect on their followers."
   You can use a more sophisticated synonym for "followed" such as "admired", "emulated", or "idolized".

3. "For instance, the increasing change in fashion and the lifestyle of the youth, is caused by the specialist sports person because of their renowned status."
   The phrase "increasing change" is redundant. You can simply say "the changing fashion and lifestyle of the youth". Additionally, it would be more accurate to say that athletes "influence" rather than "cause" these changes.

4. "Thus, it this clear that how important role they can play in order to alter the attitude of people."
   The phrase "alter the attitude of people" can be improved by using "shape" or "influence" instead of "alter", and "individuals" or "society" instead of "people".

5. "On the other hand, there are people, who argue that this saying is not right..."
   Instead of "people who argue", you can use "critics" or "opponents" to make the sentence more precise.

6. "However, this is not always true, because it is difficult to control the youngster from adopting their style as a fan can do anything for his / her followers."
   You can rephrase this sentence for clarity and coherence, e.g., "However, this notion is flawed since it is challenging to control young individuals from emulating their style, as fans are often willing to do anything for their role models."

7. "Thus, this thought is refused because of such reason."
   The word "refused" doesn't fit well here. You could replace it with "dismissed", "rejected" or "disregarded". Additionally, you can specify the "reason" you are referring to by saying "due to these reasons" or "for these aforementioned reasons".

Overall, your essay shows a good range of vocabulary
