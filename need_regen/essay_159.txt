Topic:

"Some people say that we do not need printed newspaper anymore. To what extent do you agree or disagree ?"

Essay:

"The advent of technology has led to an upsurge in digital media, which has given rise to the belief that traditional printed newspapers have become dispensable. While some individuals may still prefer the traditional means of acquiring news, I am of the opinion that the role of online news is more significant since it provides instant information and benefits the environment. 

Due to the speed with which they could be updated, I believe that e-news would be superior to conventional ones. Internet users can access the most recent news within minutes as a result of real-time updates on local and international occurrences. For example, today's people can access global updates instantly on the internet or other social media, namely Facebook or Tiktok to keep themselves abreast of the latest news. In contrast, regarding the time-consuming nature of newspaper editing and publication, readers will have to wait several days before they can obtain the news relevant to some staggering events.

 I suppose that, in addition to the high-speed update of information, online news sources will eventually replace traditional newspapers because of their environmental benefits. In terms of the environment, the production of newspapers, which requires a large amount of paper, ink, and delivery truck distribution, the widespread adoption of e-news would save numerous trees from being cut down and reduce air pollution. On the other hand, with the increasing number of people in modern society advocating eco-friendly lifestyles, the future of conventional printed newspapers does not seem to have much potential when compared with the convenient presence of online news.

In conclusion, I believe that paper-based articles are becoming obsolete and unnecessary due to the increasing popularity of e-news available on different social media platforms. This is a beneficial source for today's users to obtain instant information since it can provide immediate details of the latest events and help preserve natural resources."


Revised:

The emergence of digital media has sparked a debate regarding the relevance of printed newspapers in the present era. While some people argue that traditional newspapers are no longer necessary, others still prefer the conventional way of accessing news. In my opinion, online news is more advantageous as it offers instantaneous information and has a positive impact on the environment.

One of the advantages of e-news is that it is faster and more up-to-date than traditional newspapers. With just a few clicks, internet users can access the latest news and stay informed about global events. Compared to the time-consuming process of editing and publishing newspapers, online news sources are updated in real-time. As a result, readers can obtain breaking news within minutes of an event occurring.

In addition to providing speedy access to news, online news is also significantly more environmentally friendly than printed newspapers. The production of newspapers requires a large amount of paper, ink, and delivery trucks, leading to deforestation and air pollution. In contrast, online news sources are paperless and have a minimal impact on the environment. Given the current worldwide movement towards preserving natural resources and adopting eco-friendly lifestyles, the use of e-news is becoming increasingly popular.

In conclusion, although some people still prefer the traditional means of accessing news, the benefits of online news are undeniable. The fast, up-to-date, and environment-friendly nature of e-news sources makes them the preferred option for many people. Therefore, it is safe to say that the use of printed newspapers is becoming outdated and unnecessary in today's society.
