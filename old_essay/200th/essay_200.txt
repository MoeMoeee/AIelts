Essay:

"Currently, people are prone to be fading away and even be jeopardized by conventional architectures. Hence, there are several purposes for museums and galleries and a series of steps being taken are expected to preserve and strengthen the importance of these traditional buildings.
At the outset, the main function of museums and galleries is to demonstrate local citizens the fact regarding their ancestors’ lifestyle, traditional heritages, as well as local cultures. Encountering these potential targets, the governments have to take steps to preserve and upgrade these traditional values. Moreover, it could raise a citizen’s awareness of traditional identities. Besides, in terms of educating the next generation well, the government should spare no efforts to set up an educational fund. Authorities, for instance, could organize adolescences to visit it, and advocate that only by face-to-face could they appreciate the real values of culture and spirits in their country, and enjoy the historical and aesthetic atmosphere in the conventional architectures.
In my conclusion, without these conventional architectures, we would lose valuable treasures and are likely to be at a loss under the background of globalization when overseas cultures intrude. Consequently, nothing is more important to make a considerable investment fund on traditional buildings."

Revised:

In recent times, museums and galleries have gained significant importance. These places serve multiple purposes, and their preservation and funding are crucial. In this essay, I will discuss the reasons for the significance of museums and the steps that can be taken to fund them.

Museums and galleries play a vital role in educating people about their cultural heritage, ancestors’ lifestyle, and local traditions. They also provide an opportunity for children to learn and understand the sacrifices made by their predecessors. Consequently, museums can help cultivate a sense of patriotism. Additionally, these traditional buildings can boost a country's image and promote its economy by attracting tourists interested in local architecture and history.

To preserve the importance of museums, governments need to take certain steps. Firstly, they can invest in social media platforms, such as television and newspapers, to promote the image of these buildings. This would highlight the merits museums bring while restoring their original appearance. Furthermore, the government should encourage citizens to realize the importance of traditional identities. They could organize visits to museums for teenagers and promote face-to-face interactions for appreciating the true values of culture and spirits in their country.

In conclusion, traditional buildings like museums and galleries are valuable treasures that need to be preserved. Failure to do so could result in a loss of heritage and even losing oneself amidst globalized cultures. Therefore, considerable investment funds must be made to protect and maintain the significance of these buildings.
