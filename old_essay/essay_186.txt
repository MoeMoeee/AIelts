Topic:

"people have difference visions about shopping online some of them encourage and others says its disadvantage outweigh their advantage, what is your opinion?"

Essay:

"Trading on the Internet has become more prevalent since the pandemic. Some traditional dealers believe it can cause detrimental impacts on the existing stores on the street so online shops should be limited rigidly. However, I believe the merits of trading online override the demerits and I will elaborate on the reasons in the following essay.

To commence, shopping online saves transportation efforts for both customers and sellers. For example, customers can browse the products they need through the website whenever they want. They do not need to travel distances to the stores in person. Similarly, the dealers do not need to stand in their stores and wait for their buyers in a fixed business hour. They only move the merchandise from the stocks to delivery when the order is made. 

Nevertheless, some traditional sellers argue that online shops have threatened the existing commercial models and raised countless trade disputes. Although the value of the products can be difficult to be estimated on the Internet, the Taiwanese government has already provided online shoppers with a complete resolution. Thus, the customers can return the products unconditionally within one week. Taking that risks into consideration, the online dealers might have risen the prices of the goods, but the price will strike the balance again in the market eventually.

In conclusion, despite some disputes in trading, online shops provide not only convenience but also  economic efficiency in transportation. As a result, I believe this shopping model should be encouraged and such a shopping habit will be widely adopted by the general public."


Revised:

The popularity of online shopping has increased significantly during the pandemic. While some people support this practice, others believe that it has a greater number of disadvantages compared to advantages. In my opinion, the benefits of online shopping outweigh its drawbacks, and I will elaborate on my viewpoint in this essay.

To begin with, one of the primary advantages of shopping online is the convenience it offers. With just a few clicks on the computer or phone, the customer can browse products and make a purchase without having to leave their home. This can save time and energy, particularly for people with busy schedules. Online shopping also eliminates the need for physical stores, which means that customers can shop at any time of the day or night, making it especially appealing to those who work late nights or long hours.

However, some people argue that online shopping has a detrimental impact on traditional brick-and-mortar stores. They claim that online shopping threatens their commercial models by attracting customers away from physical stores. Additionally, some online shops have reputations for engaging in unfair business practices, such as false advertising. These issues have led to numerous trade disputes between online retailers and traditional stores.

In conclusion, while critics of online shopping cite numerous concerns, including unfair business practices and a negative impact on traditional stores, I believe that the convenience and cost-effectiveness it offers more than compensate for these drawbacks. As a result, I think that online shopping should be encouraged and that it will continue to be an increasingly popular way of shopping for consumers in the future.
