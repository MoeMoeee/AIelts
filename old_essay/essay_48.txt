Revised:

In today's technological era, computers have become an integral part of our lives, with children being no exception. The topic of whether using a computer every day has more negative than positive effects on young children is a controversial issue. In my opinion, the positive impacts of computer use outweigh the negative ones.

Firstly, computers help children with their studies. By accessing the internet, they can search for information related to their studies, which helps to enhance their knowledge and broaden their understanding of various topics. Secondly, computers help to improve their skills, particularly their communication skills. Through social media platforms like Facebook and Twitter, young children can interact with their friends and relatives, which enhances their communication capabilities. They also become proficient at using computers, which can be useful in their future careers. Additionally, organizations can benefit from having computer-literate employees that require less training.

However, there are some negative effects associated with using computers every day. One significant disadvantage is the health problems it can cause among young children, such as eye strain and obesity resulting from prolonged sitting. Furthermore, excessive computer use can result in children neglecting interaction with their parents or guardians, which can lead to a communication gap.

In conclusion, while there are pros and cons to using computers every day, parents should set limits on the amount of time their children spend on computers and monitor the websites they are accessing. Overall, computer use has numerous benefits for young children, and the negative effects can be minimized if proper guidelines are followed.
