Topic:

"Some people prefer to live in a house, while others feel that there are more advantages to living in an apartment. Are there more advantages than disadvantages of living in a house compared with living in an apartment?"

Essay:

"In current world, people tend to give a lot of thoughts on choosing their preferred home. While some people prefers to live in a house, others find flats more accommodating. I am of the opinion that living in a house is more advantageous in relation to staying in an apartment. I will discuss negatives and positives of living in a house in relevance to that in an apartment in this essay. 

Firstly, owning and maintaining a house is relatively expensive, and usually less affordable if anyone wants to have it closer to the city, whereas flats are comparatively cheaper. For example, purchasing a land near the city is often expensive, and in addition to this there is a huge building cost while the purchasing and building cost divides among a number of tenants. Besides this, there is greater risk of security lies in houses. This is because in apartments motilities of outsiders is constantly monitored, and residents mostly have greater control on whom to let in, and on the other hand anyone can easily forcibly enter into in a house.

However, houses offer more openness, freedom and fresh air as these usually have a front lawn where residents can enjoy warm mornings and evenings. In contrast, apartments are usually closed or have small balconies, and thus people tend to feel secluded in them. Moreover, houses are usually more customizable as they are often build on the request of the owner, and also can be easily rebuilt. This provides residents more maneuverability to design and rebuild their house as they want.

In conclusion, although apartments are often cheaper and more secured as compared to houses, I feel living in a house is more beneficial as they offer more openness, freedom and comfortable mornings and evenings, and are easier to redesign and rebuild."

Revised:

In today's society, individuals carefully consider their living arrangements. While some individuals prefer living in a house, others find apartments more desirable. In this essay, I will discuss the advantages and disadvantages of living in a house compared to an apartment.

On the one hand, owning and maintaining a house can be relatively expensive, especially if the house is located in a city center. In contrast, apartments tend to be cheaper due to the building cost being divided among a number of tenants. Additionally, living in a house carries a greater risk of security issues as outsiders have easier access. Conversely, individuals living in an apartment have the benefit of constant monitoring of who enters the building, making it more secure.

On the other hand, houses provide more openness, freedom, and fresh air as most houses have a front lawn where residents can enjoy the outdoors. In contrast, apartments tend to be more closed off and may have limited outdoor space, leading to feelings of isolation. Moreover, houses are typically more customizable as they can be built to suit individual needs and preferences.

In conclusion, while apartments can be more affordable and secure than houses, living in a house offers more freedom and a closer connection with nature. Furthermore, houses provide more opportunities for customization and can be rebuilt more easily. Overall, it ultimately depends on individual preferences and circumstances when choosing between living in a house or an apartment.
