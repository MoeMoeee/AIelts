For example, the former governor of Delta State believes that success comes from moving forward and not dwelling on the past.

On the other hand, those who support improving a bad situation assert that it helps individuals achieve better outcomes in the long term. By acknowledging the problem and working towards a solution, individuals gain a better understanding of what went wrong and avoid making the same mistake in the future. For instance, the popular artist Tiwa Savage revealed in a television interview that continual improvement contributed to her success.

In conclusion, while it is understandable that some people advocate accepting and moving on from a bad situation, improving the situation is necessary to understand how to tackle the problem better if it resurfaces. Therefore, this essay recommends working towards a solution rather than accepting the status quo.
