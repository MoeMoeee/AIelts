Topic:

"Some people think that professional athletes make good role models for young people, while others believe they don’t.

Discuss both these points of views and give your own opinion."

Essay:

"Every famous person's activity has an effect somewhere on other people. Some people say that professional players make good role models for the younger generation, while others think that this is not right. Through this essay I will discuss both of the points in detail and then will come to a reasonable conclusion comprising of my opinion.

Famous players can play a vital role in the youth development. They are followed by many people, because of their reputation and hence their every action has a direct effect on their followers. For instance, the increasing change in fashion and the lifestyle of the youth, is caused by the specialist sports person because of their renowned status. Thus, it this clear that how important role they can play in order to alter the attitude of people.

On the other hand, there are people, who argue that this saying is not right, because they believe that every person is capable enough to make the difference between right and wrong. It is further added that, though, such distinguished players have the reputation and respect in society, but this does not mean that they must be followed in every moment of life. However, this is not always true, because it is difficult to control the youngster from adopting their style as a fan can do anything for his / her followers. Thus, this thought is refused because of such reason.

In conclusion, it is clear that professional sports persons can play a vital role in order to affect the lives of youth positively, because they have fans and followers everywhere. Thus, I believe that it is right to say that esteemed sports person can make a good role model for the younger generation of a community."


Revised:

It is often debated whether professional athletes are good role models for young people. While some argue that their fame and status make them influential figures, others believe that individuals should be able to distinguish right from wrong without external influence. In this essay, both perspectives will be examined before presenting a personal opinion.

On one hand, professional athletes can have a positive impact on the development of young people. Due to their celebrity status, they have a significant influence on the behavior and attitudes of their followers. For example, they can inspire individuals to pursue active and healthy lifestyles or even instill values such as hard work and determination. Therefore, it is clear that sports figures can play a crucial role in shaping the character of young people.

On the other hand, there are those who contend that individuals should not rely on celebrity figures as role models. They argue that every person has the capacity to differentiate between right and wrong and that children should be taught this skill. Moreover, they believe that blindly following the behavior of athletes can result in poor decision-making and negative outcomes. However, this argument fails to acknowledge the significant influence that athletes can have on impressionable young people.

In conclusion, it is evident that professional athletes possess the ability to shape the values and behaviors of young people. While it is important to ensure that children learn to distinguish between right and wrong independently, it cannot be denied that athletes have a significant impact on the impressionable youth. Therefore, it can be concluded that professional athletes can serve as positive role models for younger generations.
