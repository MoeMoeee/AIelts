"Nowadays, the change of world temperature is a controversial issue. While it is believed that many businesses could be destroyed by fixing global warming, I side with those who feel that solving this problem is a chance for many businesses. 

On the one hand, it is argued that addressing the climate change could make some people unemployed. Those in favour of this idea state that one of the main sources of global warming is big factories using fossil as a source of energy to make their products. Furthermore, climate change reforms could have a positive effect on the car industry. Car companies could use a new technology to make electric cars which are more efficient and less pollutant for the environment. Since people try to have a role in improving environmental issues, they are more interested in buying electric cars which leads to more success for this business. 

In conclusion, although climate change reforms might shut down some old building industries or car companies, it could succeed businesses using renewable and electric energy. Therefore, on balance, I believe reforms resulted from climate change efforts have positive impacts on businesses."


Revised:

Nowadays, the issue of climate change is highly debated. While some argue that climate change reforms can have a negative impact on businesses, I am of the opinion that they present an opportunity for businesses. 

On the one hand, it is believed that addressing climate change can result in job losses. Advocates of this view state that one of the main contributors to global warming is large factories that use fossil fuels as an energy source for their production processes. For example, the building industry consumes tremendous amounts of gasoline to manufacture bricks. Therefore, banning the use of this fuel could lead to the closure of many factories, consequently leaving workers unemployed. Moreover, opponents of climate change reforms argue that scrapping old cars, which are major contributors to air pollution and carbon dioxide emissions, could lead to job losses for car drivers and disrupt transportation systems. 

However, there are numerous opportunities for businesses in combating climate change. The development of new environmental-friendly energy sources is gaining popularity, which will increase the need for suppliers to expand their businesses to meet the growing demands. For instance, the establishment of new power plants that rely on renewable energy, such as wind power, can create numerous job opportunities. Additionally, climate change reforms can have a positive impact on the car industry. By adopting new technologies and producing electric cars that are more efficient and less polluting, car companies can cater to the increasing demand for environmentally-friendly alternatives. As people become more concerned about addressing environmental issues, the demand for electric cars will rise, providing businesses with greater success. 

In conclusion, while climate change reforms may lead to the closure of certain industries and car companies, they also offer opportunities for businesses that embrace renewable and electric energy alternatives. Therefore, considering the overall impact, I believe that climate change reforms have a positive effect on businesses.
