So, companies from the lower scale to affluent companies are spending a lot of money in advertising. I do not completely accord with this point because quality and purity products would popular automatically.

In conclusion, marketing is one of the best and easiest ways to bring success for the company but without any advertisements also we can get success as well."


Revised:

In today's fast-paced world, entrepreneurs often hinge their success on promoting their products. While many businesses, from start-ups to major corporations, invest heavily in advertising, I disagree with the notion that marketing and promotion are the only keys to success. I believe that high-quality and pure products will naturally gain popularity among consumers.

On one hand, companies with mesmerizing advertisements attract a vast number of customers to their shops. Advertisements also educate people about the products and their uses. Consequently, people become more brand-conscious and even prefer buying costly branded items. These factors contribute to the growth of large-scale businesses, ultimately benefiting the companies. For example, Nike, Puma, and Rolex are some of the most popular and successful companies in the world, and their products appear on our TV screens every day.

On the other hand, some brands require no promotion yet still become popular due to their high quality and reasonable cost. For instance, Lamborghini is a dream car for many, yet the company does not invest in advertising on television. A product or company can attain success by creating a unique and high-quality masterpiece that naturally garners publicity worldwide.

In conclusion, marketing and promotion can aid in bringing success to a company, but it is not the only key factor. A high-quality and unique product that satisfies consumer demand can be just as effective in achieving success.
