Essay:

"These days it is becoming common for each person to spend much less time with their household, compared with the past. This is happening due to some reasons ranging from addiction to technology to overworking. This essay will focus on the situation and provide the consequences caused by this.
First of all, the main cause of this, in my perspective, is overworking. Because the majority of people work more hours at their workplace, they have a very little amount of time to be together with their family members. The second reason of this is the fact that most people nowadays are addicted to social media. They are spending much of their time sitting in front of notebook or chatting online, which wastes their precious time.
Regarding the effects, the situation is resulting in lack of time of people to hang out with their relatives, which is especially essential for children. This could lead youngsters to feel separateness from family, and might influence badly on their upbringing. The second cause is that they might be less social as a result of getting obsessed with work. If they are overworked constantly, they might become isolated from people.
In conclusion, the main reason of being less with family is working too much and social media. There are two possible effects of this, including lack of time of humans and lack of sociality."


Revised:

In many countries around the world, people are spending less time with their families. This is a growing trend that can be attributed to a range of factors, including addiction to technology and overworking. In this essay, I will examine the reasons for this trend and discuss its effects.

One of the primary reasons for families spending less time together is the prevalence of overworking. With many people working longer hours than ever before, there is less time available for families to come together and spend quality time with one another. Another factor is the widespread addiction to social media and other technologies. Individuals today are spending more and more time browsing the internet or chatting with friends online, often at the expense of spending time with their family.

The effects of this trend are significant. Children, in particular, may feel the effects of not spending enough time with family members. Lack of time with parents and siblings can lead to feelings of isolation and disconnection, which may have a negative impact on their overall development and well-being. Moreover, when individuals work too much, they may become socially isolated, which can also impact family relationships and friendships.

In conclusion, there are numerous reasons why families are spending less time together in today's society. Whether it is due to overworking or addiction to technology, the effects are clear. Family breakdowns can have long-lasting negative consequences on individuals and society as a whole, and it is essential that families make an effort to prioritize time spent together. By committing to spending quality time with one another, we can help ensure that families remain strong and connected.
