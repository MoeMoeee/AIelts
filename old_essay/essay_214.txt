Nowadays, the business sector has become increasingly competitive, requiring workers to possess a range of skills to succeed. In my opinion, while good qualifications are important, having social skills is more vital in this century. In this essay, I will explain the benefits of being socially adept in business competition and provide a logical conclusion.

On the other hand, research has shown that individuals with introverted tendencies and good qualifications often struggle to find work. Their lack of communication skills hinders their ability to showcase their talents in their respective fields. As a result, their self-confidence may be undermined, and they may become more socially withdrawn over time. Business is a highly competitive environment, and individuals who struggle with social anxiety find it increasingly difficult to navigate these demanding waters.

In conclusion, while both qualifications and social skills are important in the competitive job market, the advantage of having strong social connections outweighs the benefits of being highly educated.
