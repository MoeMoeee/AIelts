Topic:

"Some people think that it is more beneficial to take part in sports which are played in teams, like football, while other people think that taking part in individual sports, like tennis or swimming, is better. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.
"

Essay:

"As a human being who wants to get fit from time to time, sports play a big role to make us healthy. There are numerous type of sports in this world, yet, it can be divided into two sections, individual and team sports. While there are still debates over which type is better, here I would like to describe my views on both types and choose which one is better. 

Swimming, tennis, or even running are several sports that consider as individual sports. In this type of sports, our success is highly dependent on our performance and determination to achieve our goals. If the athletes want to be the winner, they must train harder than anyone else and set the goal as high as they can. Our achievements are not relying on outside factors. For example, Usain Bolt as a runner from Jamaica, always comes to the training earlier and trains as hard as he could. Therefore he achieves several medals at the Olympics. 

On the other hand, team sports such as basketball and football  count heavily on teamwork. Even though each individual has tremendous skills, if they cannot work as a team, their team will fail. As social creatures, human cannot life on their own, our life will be depends on someone. Therefore, teamwork will be one of the key for us to survive not only in sports, but also in our entire life. 

To summarize, both individual and team sports have their own benefits, however, to achieve more in sports and also in our life, I rather to choose team sports.
"


Revised:

In today's health-conscious world, people often participate in sports to maintain physical fitness. Sports can be categorized into two types, individual and team sports. While some individuals believe that team sports like football are more beneficial, others contend that individual sports like tennis or swimming are better. In this essay, we will explore both views and then argue that one type of sport is more advantageous.

Individual sports such as running, swimming, and tennis require a high level of individual performance and personal ambition to achieve success. In these sports, an athlete's success primarily depends on their hard work and dedication. For instance, Usain Bolt from Jamaica, known for his exceptional running abilities, trained vigorously to win multiple medals at the Olympics. Nevertheless, individual sports can be solitary and lack the social interaction that is necessary for human well-being.

Unlike individual sports, team sports such as basketball or soccer demand coordinated teamwork and collaboration. In these sports, a team's success is dependent on the collaboration and cooperation of its players. Team sports promote social interaction, which builds comradery and communication skills. By learning how to work together to accomplish shared goals, athletes develop essential skills for life beyond the playing field.

To conclude, both types of sports offer different benefits. While individual sports develop personal achievement, team sports teach essential interpersonal skills. Yet, for a well-rounded experience and greater social interaction, I believe that team sports are more beneficial. In short, which type of sport one chooses is a matter of personal preference, as both have distinct advantages.
