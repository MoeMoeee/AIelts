As a result, they may prioritize their focus on these areas. Meanwhile, women are typically expected to fulfill the role of caring for the children and taking care of the household chores such as cooking, cleaning, and laundry. Conversely, mature individuals believe that men should contribute equally to the household tasks in order to provide support and alleviate the burden on women. For instance, some of my colleagues have encountered problems when they fail to share the household workload, leading to an increased burden on women.

Therefore, it is imperative for men to acknowledge the difficulties faced by women and provide their utmost support in maintaining a balanced lifestyle within the home.
