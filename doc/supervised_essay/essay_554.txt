Topic:

"Some children spen hours every day on their smartphones. 

Why is this the case? Do you think this is a positive or a negative development?"

Essay:

"In today’s world, the use of smartphones is very common, particularly among the young generation. Spending many hours on mobile phones has become a daily routine for some children. In my view, this is concerning and can be deemed a negative development due to the lack of creativity and potential hindrance in brain development. This essay will explore the reasons behind this case.

Nowadays, smartphones play an important role in people's lives. They serve not only as tools of communication but also as channels for online exploration. The perspectives on using phones freely vary depending on families and situations. Some parents might give permission to their kids for study purposes only because they could use the internet to find unlimited knowledge regardless of going to school. Others may allow phone usage for entertainment, such as watching cartoons on Youtube, as they believe it helps with relaxation.

From my perspective, it is essential to recognize the potential drawbacks associated with uncontrolled phone usage. One significant issue is that when children spend more time on phones than going out to play with friends, they may lose social skills, which can lead to problems in their adulthood. Another adverse effect is that excessive phone use might cause health problems. Numerous studies suggest that prolonged screen time can directly impact concentration and memorization, especially in the younger generation who are increasingly reliant on smartphones.

In conclusion, excessive focus on screens every day can lead to adverse consequences for children. As a result, using smartphones for specific purposes should be allowed and monitored by parents to ensure their kids spend time online in an appropriate way."


Revised:

In today's modern world, smartphones have become an integral part of our lives, especially among the younger generation. It is a common sight to see children spending hours on their smartphones every day. In my opinion, this is a concerning trend and can be considered a negative development due to its potential drawbacks, such as a lack of creativity and hindrance in brain development. This essay will delve into the reasons behind this phenomenon.

Nowadays, smartphones play a crucial role in our daily lives. They not only serve as tools for communication but also as gateways to the online world. The permissibility of smartphone usage varies among families, with some parents allowing their children to use them for educational purposes, enabling them to access unlimited knowledge on the internet without relying solely on traditional education. On the other hand, some parents allow smartphones for entertainment, such as watching cartoons on YouTube, as they believe it helps with relaxation.

However, it is important to recognize the potential drawbacks associated with unregulated smartphone usage among children. Firstly, excessive use of smartphones can diminish children's social skills if they spend more time on their phones than engaging in outdoor activities with friends. This can lead to difficulties in forming and maintaining relationships later in life. Moreover, prolonged and excessive screen time can have detrimental effects on children's health. Numerous studies have indicated that extended exposure to screens can negatively impact concentration and memory, particularly in the younger generation who are increasingly reliant on smartphones.

In conclusion, excessive use of smartphones on a daily basis among children can have adverse consequences. Therefore, it is essential for parents to monitor and regulate their children's smartphone usage, allowing it only for specific purposes and ensuring they spend their online time responsibly. This will help mitigate the potential negative effects and ensure that children strike a balance between the use of smartphones and engaging in other activities that foster their well-rounded development.
