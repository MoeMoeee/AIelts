Revised:

Nowadays, the use of technology in education is becoming increasingly common. While some people believe this is a positive trend, others argue that it is leading to negative consequences. In my opinion, I believe that technology brings positive impacts to education. 

On the one hand, technology helps students improve their abilities. In the classroom, many subjects require advanced technology to enhance learning, such as science. By incorporating technology in the class, teachers can employ various creative teaching methods. For instance, students can witness the real process of photosynthesis through the use of advanced technology. This hands-on approach allows students to grasp complex concepts more effectively, leading to academic success.

On the other hand, some argue that technology can replace the teacher's role in the classroom. With the increasing reliance on technology, teachers may have less interaction with their students. Many activities and explanations can now be provided through technology, potentially diminishing the teacher's role as the central figure in the class. For example, teachers can simply show a video of cell reproduction without having to personally explain the topic. Consequently, teachers may lose their impact as the primary source of knowledge and guidance.

In conclusion, the use of technology in the classroom has sparked controversy in society. While some believe that it may have negative effects, such as replacing the role of teachers, I am firmly convinced that incorporating technology in education can be beneficial. It helps students understand complex concepts, which in turn improves their academic performance. Therefore, I believe that technology should continue to be embraced as an essential tool for enhancing the educational experience.
