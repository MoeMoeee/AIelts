The curriculum is a vital part of education and it must be decided with extreme caution. Some people believe that sports subjects are useless and should not be a part of the future curriculum. However, I argue that sports are crucial for a balanced life, so I disagree with the notion that sports subjects should not be a part of the curriculum. This essay intends to discuss the importance of sports in anyone's life.

There are myriad advantages of a sports curriculum as it significantly affects the health of human beings. For example, research in the United States has shown that students who are enrolled in a school with a sports curriculum demonstrate more effective results compared to those students who attend traditional schools. Thus, this example illustrates the importance of sports in one's life. Without such sports practices, we may turn our new generations into robots as they may not be able to live lively lives without fears.

Many advanced nations understand the importance of sports subjects and encourage their population to practice sports on a daily basis by introducing free sports clubs. For instance, Japan has introduced various free sports clubs for schools where students can practice and get mentally refreshed, although they may feel a little tired. As a result, they become more productive.

Following this line of thought, it is evident that sports play a significant role in human life. The need arises for sports curriculum to be implemented as a necessary course in all institutions. I hope that, for the betterment of humanity, this can occur as soon as possible.
