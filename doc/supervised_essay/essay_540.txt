In the modern era, the majority of students are occupied with their academic lives and find little time for external sports activities, which are vital to their overall development. Some individuals believe that it is imperative that children participate in club athletics, while others disagree. In this essay, I will examine both perspectives and offer my opinion.

Firstly, athletic activities are essential for the physical and mental development of children. In today's world, where the majority of adolescents remain indoors after school or spend their time on mobile devices or watching television, engaging in some form of athletic activity is crucial for their overall well-being. Team sports teach them how to collaborate and work together in a club setting. Additionally, they have the opportunity to develop leadership abilities by serving as team leaders and learn how to motivate team members in low morale situations, thereby enhancing their overall teamwork and coordination. According to a recent survey conducted by the sports council of India, it was found that teenagers who participate in sports alongside their education have greater stamina, focus, and concentration than their peers.

On the other hand, opponents of team sports argue that they are a waste of time that could be better spent on academic pursuits. They believe that only the team leader receives recognition for the overall performance, leaving talented individuals with limited opportunities to advance. Additionally, critics claim that participating in sports may distract students from their studies, leading to a loss of focus and subpar academic performance.

In conclusion, after weighing the pros and cons, I am of the opinion that club sports are crucial for children's career success, and they should participate in some form of physical activity. Engaging in team sports not only contributes to their physical and mental development but also teaches them important life skills such as collaboration, leadership, and motivation. It is essential to strike a balance between academics and sports so that children can excel in both areas and lead well-rounded lives.
