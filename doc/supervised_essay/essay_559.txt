Topic:

"as well as making money businesses also have aresponsibility towards society. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"

Essay:

"It is believed that rather than focusing only on gaining profit, organisations should contribute and should adhere to corporate responsibility towards the community. I completely agree with the aforementioned notion and the reasons will be discussed in the upcoming paragraphs.

To begin with, there are a plethora of causes for the agreement with the above-asserted statement. In the modern era, big companies generally focus only on piling up money. However, they do not provide quality in return to the individuals. To be more precise, these corporations gain success only because of the public and once they get successful they forget to provide good quality  and authentic products in a race to earn heaps of money. Therefore, they should be involved in socio-economic activities. It is also essential for them to earn profit in order to run their business in an adequate manner.

Probing further, as these companies earn a large amount of money, they can help to eradicate poverty from society. In other words, these huge companies can provide employment to individuals and should help them financially by giving an appropriate amount of salary according to their work. While a company is financially stable, it should be indulged in charitable social activities .For instance, during the corona pandemic, tata company supported their employees by providing them with adequate amounts for their survival in that challenging situation. 

In conclusion, I would like to reiterate that although it is necessary for a company to earn money to manage their livelihood, they should provide people with desired quality  according to the price and help the community through donation practices."


Revised:

It is believed that businesses have a responsibility towards society and should not only focus on making money. I completely agree with this notion and will discuss the reasons in the following paragraphs.

Firstly, many large corporations prioritize profit accumulation over providing quality products or services to the public. Once they achieve success, they tend to neglect the importance of delivering authentic and good quality products, solely focusing on maximizing their profits. This approach neglects the well-being of consumers and fails to fulfill their needs. Therefore, it is crucial for businesses to engage in socio-economic activities and prioritize the delivery of quality goods and services while still maintaining profitability.

Furthermore, prosperous companies have the potential to make a significant impact on society by helping to alleviate poverty. They can achieve this by creating job opportunities and offering fair salaries to their employees. When companies are financially stable, they should engage in charitable activities that benefit the community. For example, during the COVID-19 pandemic, Tata Company supported its employees by providing them with adequate financial support to sustain themselves during the challenging situation. Such practices not only enhance the company's reputation but also contribute positively to society.

In conclusion, while it is essential for businesses to earn profits to sustain themselves, they should also prioritize providing high-quality products and services to consumers. Furthermore, they should contribute to the betterment of society through charitable actions and support for those in need. By adhering to corporate responsibility, businesses can create a balance between profitability and societal well-being.
