Revised:

Nowadays, young generations are idolizing media and sports stars, even though they do not set a positive example. This essay argues that this trend is detrimental as it can lead young people to emulate negative behaviors and negatively impact their academic performance.

One main concern is that media and sports stars often exhibit inappropriate actions, which can influence impressionable minds, especially among young people. To boost their ratings, the media often airs scandalous gossip that piques curiosity, particularly when it involves underage content. For instance, TikTok, a popular social media platform, features tiktokers donning revealing attire, which is inappropriate for underage viewers. Consequently, young children may become curious and start searching for similar content, potentially leading them towards an addiction to age-restricted videos.

Another issue is that young individuals may imitate the inappropriate habits and behaviors of their favorite athletes, consequently displaying a negative attitude towards their school activities. For example, some students may see smoking as a cool action due to its portrayal by their athletic idols. This behavior can impact their studies and overall school performance, ultimately resulting in academic penalties or even dropout.

In conclusion, media and sports stars often exert a negative influence on young generations. This influence may begin with a sense of curiosity but can quickly escalate into an addiction to inappropriate content. Additionally, young individuals may mimic unfavorable actions such as smoking, leading to disruptions in their school activities and potentially causing them to abandon their education. Therefore, it is crucial to emphasize positive role models and encourage young people to follow examples that promote personal growth and success.
