Revised:

Due to improvements in living standards, people are now living longer lives. However, the resulting aging population has led to debates regarding the impact it has on society. While some argue that it causes significant problems, others believe that it brings various advantages to businesses, governments, and society as a whole. In this essay, I will discuss both perspectives and provide my own opinion.

On one hand, there are valid concerns about the challenges posed by an aging population. One major drawback is the shortage of young labor force. As the number of young individuals declines, there is a decrease in the number of newborn babies each year. Moreover, elderly individuals often avoid bearing children due to physical reasons. As a result, recruitment agencies face a limited pool of potential employees. This shortage of young workers can have a detrimental effect on the economy, especially in industries that rely on energetic and dedicated labor force to meet the demands of society. For instance, the service industry requires a substantial number of workers to function effectively.

Furthermore, the aging population places a significant burden on the economy by necessitating increased retirement pension provisions. With a scarcity of young workers, there is a decrease in overall income for the country. The potential of a nation is often evaluated based on its workforce and economic sustainability. Insufficient funds can lead to an inability to provide adequate social welfare payments, exacerbating the financial strain. Additionally, a lack of resources diminishes the attractiveness of a country for foreign investments. Without external support, innovation and progress on a national level may be hindered.

On the other hand, proponents argue that an aging population can offer several benefits. Firstly, older workers possess extensive experience and wisdom acquired over the years. This can be advantageous for businesses, as they can utilize their expertise in various sectors. Additionally, older individuals tend to have a higher disposable income, which can drive economic growth by stimulating consumer demand. Lastly, an aging population can foster a sense of solidarity and social cohesion as communities come together to support and care for older members.

However, I believe that the disadvantages of an aging population outweigh the advantages. The reduction in the young labor force and the strain on the economy are significant concerns. Without a sufficient number of young workers, industries may struggle to meet the demands of society. Additionally, the financial burden of providing retirement pensions without enough resources can lead to a decline in the overall economic growth and societal well-being.

In conclusion, while some argue that an aging population brings benefits to businesses, governments, and society as a whole, I believe that the drawbacks outweigh the advantages. The shortages in the young labor force and the strain on the economy pose significant challenges that need to be addressed. It is essential for governments and policymakers to find ways to mitigate these problems while ensuring the well-being and support of the aging population.

2. The dramatic decrease in the number of young dwellers has resulted in a reduction in the total of newborn babies per year.
   - "the total of newborn babies" cần được sửa thành "the total number of newborn babies". Hoặc cũng có thể dùng "the total count of newborn babies".

3. Alongside that, the elderly tend to avoid giving birth to an infant because their body can not accommodate the conditions of bearing a foetus.
   - "tend to avoid giving birth to an infant" có thể sửa thành "tend to not give birth to an infant" hoặc "tend to avoid having a child". "Giving birth to an infant" trùng lặp với ý trước đó trong câu.
