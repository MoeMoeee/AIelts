Topic:

"Some people prefer to spend their lives doing the same things and avoiding change. Others, however, think that the change is always a good thing. Discuss both the views and give your own opinion. Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant example from your knowledge or experience."

Essay:

"People are different from each other in many ways. One such difference is the way of spending life. While some people like to spend their life doing one job or similar routine, others prefer changes in their lives. In my opinion a continuous change in life is the way to live. In the following essay we will discuss both views.
A famous saying is “never drink from stagnant river”. Firstly, some people get bored of same routine. They need change that can either be in job or daily routine. This helps them to take their mind off certain things and explore better options for themselves. For example it is a common trend among Irish people to seek for jobs overseas. This helps them in learning the life better and find better opportunities for themselves. Secondly, some people resort to travelling and living abroad for extended periods of time. Their idea is to take a time off from the same routine and spend some time in foreign culture and understanding different ways of life. For instance many government servants take time off after retirement to travel and enjoy their lives.
On the other hand, some people prefer to follow a strict routine. This is their way of life which has discipline. Such people are generally more career focused and cannot take the risk of discontinuity. For example, If we glance at lives of Buddha monks, their way of life is everything to them. Following strict routines help them to be strong. Similarly many sportsperson follow same routine of working certain hours of the day and focusing on health in the rest of time. Hence the choice depends on individuals thinking.
In conclusion, both views have their own advantages and disadvantages, but most of it depends on personal choices and preferences. In my opinion traveling and continuous change is the way to live in the world as it gives us more chance to explore the world and find better option for our careers."


Revised:

Individuals possess differing perspectives regarding how they should structure their life. While some people enjoy carrying out the same tasks and avoiding any alterations, others perceive change as an excellent opportunity. This essay aims to explore the advantages and drawbacks of both attitudes before providing an opinion on this issue.

Some people dislike the monotonous nature of a single routine, opting to vary their daily activities to prevent boredom. For example, Irish citizens often head abroad to find new employment opportunities, exposing them to unique cultures while finding increased job offers. Additionally, working professionals who have retired from their job may travel to different countries to relax and experience a change of pace.

Meanwhile, certain people prefer the discipline inherent in following rigid schedules, believing that the consistency will ultimately lead to success. For instance, Buddhist monks structure their entire lives around set timetables to promote willpower and self-discipline. Similarly, athletes typically adhere to strict training and diet regimens to improve their physique and achieve success.

Overall, while it may seem difficult to strike a balance between change and routine, perspectives on this matter are largely subjective, being dependent on one's personal preferences. In my view, embracing continuous change and experiencing continual learning opportunities provides the best path in living a fulfilling life.
