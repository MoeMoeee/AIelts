However, by leading a society or crowd, individuals continuously develop self-confidence. This increased confidence enables leaders to make more rational decisions in stressful and challenging situations. Furthermore, this self-assuredness can ensure success for leaders, allowing them to excel and inspire others with their confidence. Consequently, being a leader also helps individuals to be more fulfilled and confident in different areas.

In conclusion, being a leader has numerous positive effects and consequences. Firstly, it allows individuals to experience freedom and independence. Secondly, it increases their confidence and determination. Personally, I would definitely prefer to be a leader.
