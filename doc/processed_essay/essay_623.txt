Revised:

These days, many people are experiencing difficulties with getting enough sleep, which is giving rise to a myriad of health and behavioral problems. This essay will discuss the main issues associated with lack of sleep and propose a potential solution.

The primary problems caused by insufficient sleep include a lack of motivation and an absence of patience. When individuals are tired, they are unable to perform at their maximum capacity and struggle to complete tasks that require concentration and effort. This can have negative consequences in the workplace, as they may lack the energy to fulfill their responsibilities effectively. For example, if someone has only had four hours of sleep, they may only be able to complete one important task out of ten, which could jeopardize their job security. Moreover, a lack of sleep can lead to impatience, anger, and heightened levels of stress. For instance, if an individual has only slept for 120 minutes, they are likely to become irritable and easily frustrated the following day.

In my opinion, the best solution to this problem is to establish a consistent sleep schedule. By monitoring the time they go to bed and wake up, individuals can become more mindful of their sleep patterns and make a conscious effort to prioritize getting enough rest. Additionally, by recognizing the correlation between their behavior and the amount of sleep they have received, they will be motivated to attain an adequate amount of sleep on a daily basis. One practical tool that can aid in this process is a sleep tracking device.

In conclusion, the lack of sufficient sleep not only results in a lack of patience but also hinders performance at work. The most effective solution is to establish a sleep schedule and use tools such as sleep trackers to monitor and improve sleep quality.
