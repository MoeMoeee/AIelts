Revised:

Nowadays, art plays an integral role in human life, as creativity is essential to support our activities. Artists and entertainers rely on their artistic skills to entertain people. Therefore, I strongly believe that art is a crucial aspect of modern society and should be incorporated into the educational curriculum from primary school to university.

To begin with, forms of entertainment, such as music and art galleries, are important for individuals to cope with the pressures and stresses of daily life. They assist in relaxation and happiness. Moreover, art also creates job opportunities for young people, as it expands the job market in creative sectors, particularly in urban areas. Consequently, the arts continue to have a significant place in our modern lifestyles.

However, in contemporary society, education has placed a greater emphasis on science and technology rather than art. Despite this, art should still be integrated into all subjects of study, ranging from primary school to university. This integration encourages creativity and cultivates a sense of craftsmanship in students, regardless of their educational background. For example, when students incorporate their artistic skills into designing user interfaces for apps, it results in visually appealing and captivating designs.

In conclusion, art cannot be separated from modern life, and it is essential to incorporate it into all levels of education, from elementary to university. Therefore, I strongly believe that artistry continues to play a vital role in making people's lives enjoyable and vibrant.
