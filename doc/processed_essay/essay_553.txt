Revised:

Nowadays, in many societies, children and teenagers are given more freedom compared to the past. While there are some drawbacks associated with this availability, I believe the main benefits are more substantial.

On the one hand, the primary advantage of having more freedom is that it allows children to utilize up-to-the-minute gadgets and have access to higher education sources, thereby reinforcing their knowledge. Furthermore, having access to technology not only provides teenagers with a wide range of information to enhance their knowledge but also helps them discover their passion for a specific subject, which can shape their future career. For example, online platforms such as YouTube can be highly beneficial for children to learn more about a particular subject that may not have been available in the past. Another benefit of freedom is that it allows youngsters to express their opinions, which contributes to the development of their personalities and boosts their self-confidence. For instance, when children are asked for their ideas and thoughts, they feel more valued and have the opportunity to accelerate their maturity process.

On the other hand, giving excessive freedom to children can give rise to major problems. One disadvantage is that when children have too much freedom, they are more likely to be influenced by peer pressure and engage in harmful behaviors such as smoking or drug abuse. Therefore, it is important for parents to allocate appropriate boundaries and raise awareness about the detrimental effects of these behaviors. Another negative aspect of giving too much freedom is that teenagers may engage in risky activities that can lead to serious consequences. For example, allowing teenagers to drive before the legal age can pose a threat to society.

In conclusion, while it is true that giving freedom to children may seem disadvantageous under certain circumstances, its positive influences, such as access to greater education and the development of self-confidence, outweigh the disadvantages. However, it is crucial for parents to strike a balance by setting appropriate boundaries and providing guidance to ensure that children make responsible choices and avoid engaging in harmful behaviors. By doing so, children can enjoy the benefits of freedom while also staying safe and protected.
