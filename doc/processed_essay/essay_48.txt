Moreover, excessive computer use can create a communication gap between children and their parents or guardians, as they spend more time engrossed in computer activities rather than engaging in face-to-face conversations.

In conclusion, while the use of computers by young children has both advantages and disadvantages, I believe that the benefits outweigh the drawbacks. It is crucial for parents to set time limits for computer usage and monitor the websites their children visit.
