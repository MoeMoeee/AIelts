Revised:

It is true that some people believe that history is the most essential subject in school, while others argue that subjects like science and technology are more important in today's world. This essay will discuss both views and provide my own opinion.

To begin with, history is a crucial subject as it allows us to learn from the past and understand the events and decisions that shaped our world. Every country has its own history, filled with wars, empires, revolutions, and triumphs. For example, in Indonesia, the long struggle for freedom is an integral part of the country's history. By studying history, students are able to gain a deeper understanding of their nation's journey toward independence and develop a sense of nationalism. History textbooks provide detailed accounts of the founders, heroes, significant places, and dates, which help to create a collective identity. This knowledge instills a sense of pride and inspires citizens to contribute to the betterment of their country.

However, in today's globalized world, science and technology play an increasingly important role. The rapid development of technology has made information accessible to everyone, irrespective of their location. People can now access up-to-date news and information through their smartphones from anywhere in the world. Moreover, technological advancements have revolutionized various sectors, from healthcare to communication and transportation. For instance, significant advancements in medicine have saved countless lives. Vaccines have helped eradicate many deadly diseases, thereby improving public health. In addition, science and technology are instrumental in solving complex problems and addressing challenges faced by societies. Countries recognize the significance of these subjects and include them in their curriculum to ensure that the younger generation is well-prepared to contribute to the ever-evolving world.

In conclusion, while history allows us to learn from the past and fosters a strong sense of national identity, science and technology are equally crucial in the modern world. History provides us with valuable lessons and serves as a reminder of our roots, whereas science and technology enable us to find solutions to contemporary problems. Both subjects contribute to the development of society and therefore should be given equal importance in the school curriculum.
