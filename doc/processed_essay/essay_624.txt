Topic:

"You and your family are moving to another country for work, and you are looking to buy a house there.

Write a letter to a property agent. In your letter:

    Introduce yourself and your family

    Describe the type of accommodation that you hope to find

    Give your preference for the location of the accommodation"

Essay:

"Dear Sir or Madam

My name is Saber Youcef, I am writing this letter to inquire about the types of accommodation that are available in your city.

I am from Algeria, me and my wife Asma, and we both work in the manufacturing industry, we are moving to London next month for work purposes, and we have been offered a 5 years contract to work for one of the biggest companies in England; thus, we started recently searching for a house there.

We are looking to purchase a two-bedroom apartment as we are expecting a child in the next few months, it would be preferable to be fully equipped, especially the kitchen for the reason that we tend to cook our own meals.

Since we would not be able to afford a car in the first few months, we would prefer to be close to the city centre; therefore, we will not face any troubles in our daily commuting.

Please look into this matter at your earliest convenience.

Yours faithfully 

Saber Youcef"


Revised:

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to inquire about the types of accommodation available in your city as my family and I are planning to move there for work purposes. 

Allow me to introduce myself and my family. My name is Saber Youcef, and I am from Algeria. I, along with my wife Asma, work in the manufacturing industry. We have recently been offered a 5-year contract to work for one of the largest companies in England, which has prompted our search for a suitable residence in your city. 

Considering we have plans to expand our family in the upcoming months, we are hoping to find a two-bedroom apartment that is fully equipped, particularly in terms of the kitchen, as we prefer to cook our own meals. 

Additionally, as we will not initially have access to a car, it would be convenient for us to be situated close to the city center. This would ensure that we face minimal difficulties during our daily commute.

I kindly request you to look into this matter at your earliest convenience.

Yours faithfully,

Saber Youcef

1. Sử dụng các từ nối dẫn vào ý đầu tiên của mỗi đoạn: Một cách tốt để tạo ra một sự liên kết mạch lạc là sử dụng các từ nối dẫn vào ý đầu tiên của mỗi đoạn. Ví dụ, bạn có thể sử dụng từ "Firstly" hoặc "To begin with" để giới thiệu ý đầu tiên của bạn trong mỗi đoạn.

2. Sử dụng các từ nối để giữ cho các ý có liên kết trơn tru: Đôi khi, các ý không được nối chặt chẽ làm cho bài viết trở nên rời rạc hoặc không mạch lạc. Bạn có thể sử dụng từ nối như "In addition", "Moreover" hoặc "Furthermore" để kết nối ý tiếp theo với ý trước đó.

Ví dụ: We are looking to purchase a two-bedroom apartment, which should be fully equipped...
