"Mobile phones have made life easier and anyone can use a mobile phone to answer or make work calls or home calls art any place 7 days a week. Do you think this development has more positive effects or negative effects on the individual and the society ?"

Essay:

"In the present world, smartphones are being used everywhere for individual purposes and also for work matters. It has been observed that they are being utilized constantly. In this article, I will talk about its drawbacks and advantages for us and also for the whole of society. 

Firstly, we should consider the fact that mobiles can help us have an easier life. Thus, it has brought many pluses. On the other hand, we should not neglect the drawbacks. Technologies, including have kept us far from each other in some ways. Moreover, using smartphones in  public can disturb others. So, this is the negative effect which we have to consider. For instance, while commuting there are a lot of people on the bus, and talking loudly in these places can harm others.

Revised:

In the present world, smartphones are being widely used for both personal and work purposes, significantly impacting our lives. This essay will discuss the positive and negative effects of mobile phones on individuals and society as a whole.

On the positive side, mobile phones have made communication more convenient and accessible for everyone. Individuals can easily connect with their loved ones at any time and from any place. In the past, it used to take months to inquire about someone's well-being, but with mobile phones, it only takes a few seconds. Furthermore, the entire society benefits from this development as companies can now address work-related issues 24/7 since employees are always accessible. It is evident that these advantages are not limited to just individuals but also extend to society as a whole.

However, there are also negative consequences that should not be overlooked. The excessive use of technology, including smartphones, has led to a decline in face-to-face interactions. Nowadays, individuals primarily rely on social media and other digital platforms for communication, resulting in weakened personal bonds and a sense of disconnection from one another. Moreover, the improper use of mobile phones in public places can cause disruptions and inconvenience others. For instance, speaking loudly on public transport can disturb fellow passengers.

In conclusion, while smartphones have undoubtedly brought many advantages by facilitating communication and enhancing accessibility, the negative aspects should also be considered. While mobile phones keep us connected and improve communication, their excessive use can lead to a decline in personal relationships and disrupt public spaces. It is essential to strike a balance and use mobile phones responsibly to ensure their positive impact on individuals and society as a whole.
