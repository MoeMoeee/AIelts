Topic:

"In spite of the advances made in agriculture, many people around the world still go hungry.

Why is this the case?

What can be done about this problem?"

Essay:

"It has been argued that many social members from all over the planet still do not have enough food despite the developments that have been made in agriculture. This phenomenon occurred because of the unpredicted rise in world population, however, there are steps that can be taken to overcome this issue and the most effective one is making a trust-worthy prognostication by means of AI and data science.

Nowadays, it is easier to raise kids compared to the old times. In addition, there are many places that are designed to take care of children or educate them when they are seven or older. That is the reason why couples find having two or three kids, worry-free and less risky. They can rely on these organizations for further assistance and bring up new family members and also work at the same time. In this way, having better circumstances to raise children has led to population growth.

Presently, we are facing a boom in technology evolution and the best example of this is Artificial Intelligence. Furthermore, there are servers which are capable of processing huge amounts of data and producing accurate results. So, by using the help of these modern options, we can train the population model on previous years' data and also predict its probable growth due to various factors, in the near future. Finally, having a point of view on how things will change can help us make better plans.

To sum up, the problem of hunger in spite of the growth in agriculture is caused by making a false assumption and it is likely to be solved by using the maximum accuracy when we decide to make future plans."


Revised:

It has been argued that despite advancements in agriculture, a significant number of individuals worldwide still suffer from hunger. This issue can be attributed to the unforeseen increase in global population. However, there are measures that can be taken to address this problem. One effective solution is the use of reliable prediction methods such as artificial intelligence (AI) and data science.

Currently, raising children has become easier compared to the past. There are numerous childcare facilities and educational institutions available for children aged seven and above. As a result, couples find it less risky and worrisome to have two or three children. They can rely on these establishments for assistance while they continue working. This improved environment for raising children has contributed to the growth of the global population.

In the present era of technological advancements, AI stands as a prime example. With servers capable of processing vast amounts of data and generating accurate results, predicting population patterns based on historical data has become feasible. By utilizing these modern tools, we can train population models and anticipate future growth based on various influencing factors. Developing an understanding of how things are likely to change can assist in creating more effective plans.

In conclusion, the problem of hunger persisting despite agricultural advancements stems from a flawed assumption about population growth. However, this issue can be resolved by employing accurate prediction methods when devising future plans. By utilizing the power of AI and data science to forecast population trends, we can effectively address the problem of hunger and ensure a brighter future for all.
