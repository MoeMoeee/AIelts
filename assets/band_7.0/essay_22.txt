Discuss both sides and give your opinion."

I will discuss both of these views and express my opinion .

There are groups of people who think that it is necessary to finish a university because without educational background there are fewer chances to get a good profession. Revised:
There are divergent opinions regarding the importance of completing university education in securing a good job. Some argue that obtaining a degree is the most effective path to becoming well-compensated, while others believe that gaining experience and developing soft skills hold greater significance. In this essay, I will discuss both perspectives and provide my own viewpoint.

On one hand, proponents of university education assert that it is imperative for individuals to attain a degree as it enhances their prospects of obtaining a lucrative profession. Universities offer a wide range of courses that equip students with essential skills relevant to their future careers. Moreover, the expertise and experience of teachers in their respective fields can greatly benefit learners. Hence, there is a belief among certain individuals that completing a university education is a crucial step for students to take.

On the other hand, proponents of the opposing view argue that practical experience and interpersonal skills are more vital than university education. They contend that even with a solid educational foundation, individuals may lack the ability to interact respectfully with others or handle challenging situations appropriately. For instance, they may struggle to handle disgruntled customers who express dissatisfaction with their purchases. Thus, these individuals emphasize the significance of gaining real-world experience rather than focusing solely on academic qualifications.

In conclusion, while there are valid arguments on both sides, I personally believe that pursuing higher education is essential for everyone. University education provides a broad range of opportunities and equips students with valuable knowledge and skills that can greatly enhance their professional prospects.
