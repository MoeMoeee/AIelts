Topic:

"The diagram below shows how solar panels can be used to provide electricity for domestic use"

Essay:

"The diagram gives information about how to produce domestic electricity by using solar energy.

There are five distinct stages to generate electricity by clean energy such as sunlight. This process begins with capturing sun rays by solar panels. However, the last final steps are about how to keep the extra domestic electricity out of the house.

First of all, solar panels on the roof of a normal house take energy from the sun and then transport this energy into DC. Next, the current  is passed to the inverter, which turns it into AC and regulates the supply of electricity. This supply goes directly to the electrical panel where the current is distributed throughout the whole house.

In the fourth step shown on the diagram, a utility meter in the home is responsible for conducting the excessive solar electricity power  outside the home through the grid. Finally, if the solar power does not provide enough supply, the electricity will flow back by the utility grid into the household through the meter."


Revised:

"The diagram illustrates the process of using solar panels to generate electricity for domestic use. There are five distinct stages involved in this process, which includes capturing sunlight, converting it into usable energy, and distributing it throughout the house.

To begin with, solar panels installed on the roof of a regular house absorb sunlight and convert it into direct current (DC) energy. Subsequently, this DC energy is passed on to an inverter, which converts it into alternating current (AC) and regulates the supply of electricity. The AC electricity then flows into the electrical panel, from where it is distributed and used throughout the entire household.

In the fourth stage depicted in the diagram, excess solar electricity that is not used by the household is directed outside the home through the utility meter and into the grid. Finally, if there is a shortage of solar power and the household requires additional electricity, it can be obtained from the utility grid through the meter.

In conclusion, solar panels play a crucial role in the generation of electricity for domestic use, starting from capturing sunlight to distributing the converted energy throughout the house. The excess solar electricity can be sent to the grid, while the additional electricity needed by the household can be obtained from the grid. This process promotes the usage of clean and renewable energy sources, reducing our reliance on fossil fuels and contributing to a more sustainable future."
