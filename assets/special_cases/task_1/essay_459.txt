Revised Essay:

The two maps illustrate the development of a specific area from 1965 to the present day.

Overall, it is evident that significant changes have taken place in the area over the given period. One noticeable transformation is the replacement of the ferry with a road bridge.

By examining the first map, it can be observed that the forest on the North-West side of the river has been cleared to create ample space for a large car park. Additionally, a considerable number of new accommodations have been built on the opposite side of the river, specifically on the right-hand side of the map. Furthermore, three blocks of apartments have been constructed near the river bank, adjacent to a small car park. Moreover, a minor road connects a small car park located centrally within the residential areas to the main road.

In contrast, the South-East of the bridge has witnessed the construction of more accommodations. Furthermore, several future developments are planned to occur by 2018. These include the construction of a parallel footbridge for pedestrians, in close proximity to the road bridge. Similarly, new car parks and a yacht marina are scheduled to be opened in the bottom right-hand corner of the map in 2018.

To conclude, considerable transformations have taken place in the area from 1965 to the present day. The replacement of the ferry with a road bridge and the development of new accommodations and car parks are the most noteworthy changes. Additionally, future plans include the construction of a footbridge, car parks, and a yacht marina.
