Topic:

"You are currently trying to improve your knowledge of a language which is not your original

language. You have a friend who speaks this new language perfectly. Write to your friend.

Explaining why you want to learn this new language

Asking him/her for suggestions on how to learn more quickly

Proposing that you meet your friend to talk about these steps

Begin your letter ‘Dear

Do not write any addresses"

Essay:

"Dear Peter

I thought it was time to get in touch, especially as I remember you’re a fluent speaker of Italian. I

might have mentioned this before, but I’m working for an Italian company now, and so I need to speak quite a lot of Italian when I have meetings with the staff.

I wonder if you could give me a few tips on how to speed up my Italian learning? Do you think I

should have lessons, or are there any self-study books which you can recommend? Sometimes I think

about doing a crash course in Italy, but I think this might be too intensive, and I’d be interested to hear your view on that too.

Maybe, if you’re going to be in the area in the next few weeks, we could get together and I could pick

your brains about this subject. There is actually a very well-known Italian restaurant in my street, so

dinner would be my pleasure, and the surroundings would be perfect! 

Let me know what you think, and I hope to see you at some point soon.

All the best,

Nestor"


Revised:

Dear Peter,

I hope this letter finds you well. It has been quite some time since we last spoke, but your fluency in Italian came to mind recently, and I believe you could greatly assist me in my quest to improve my language proficiency.

The reason behind my desire to learn Italian is related to my current employment at an Italian company. As you can imagine, it is essential for me to be able to communicate effectively with the staff, especially during meetings. Therefore, I feel the need to enhance my Italian skills.

Since you have already mastered the language, I would greatly appreciate any suggestions you may have on how to expedite my learning process. Should I consider taking formal language courses, or are there any self-study books that you could recommend? Furthermore, I have been contemplating attending an intensive crash course in Italy, but I am unsure if this would be the most effective approach. I value your opinion on this matter and would love to hear your thoughts.

If you happen to find yourself in the vicinity within the next few weeks, perhaps we could arrange a meeting to discuss these steps in person. There is a renowned Italian restaurant conveniently located on my street, which would not only provide a delightful setting but also allow us to enjoy a meal together. I would be thrilled at the opportunity to pick your brain and gain insights from your experience.

Please let me know your thoughts on this matter, and I hope to have the pleasure of seeing you soon.

Best regards,

Nestor

Lexical Resource:

Trong bài viết của bạn, Lexical Resource được sử dụng một cách hợp lý và phong phú ở mức trung bình. Dưới đây là một số lỗi sau và giải thích:

1. "...working for an Italian company now" - Xin lưu ý rằng bạn đang làm việc CHO một công ty Ý chứ không phải LÀM VIỆC cho công ty đó.

2. "I need to speak quite a lot of Italian when I have meetings with the staff." - "The staff" thường chỉ nhóm người làm việc trong một công ty cụ thể, trong trường hợp này có thể nên sử dụng "colleagues" (đồng nghiệp) thay cho "staff" (nhân viên).

3. "I wonder if you could give me a few tips on how to speed up my Italian learning?" - Sử dụng "language learning" thay cho "Italian learning" để tránh lặp lại từ "Italian" quá nhiều.

4. "Sometimes I think about doing a crash course in Italy" - Đúng ý, nhưng "crash course" có thể được giải thích rõ hơn, ví dụ: "an intensive language course".

5. "Maybe, if you’re going to be in the area in the next few weeks, we could get together and I could pick your brains about this subject." - "pick your brains" là một cụm từ thông tục có nghĩa là "hỏi ý kiến ai về một vấn đề cụ thể". Bạn có thể thay thế nó bằng một từ/ cụm từ khác để tránh sự lặp lại. 

Những từ và cụm từ bạn đã sử dụng (như "fluent speaker," "self-study books," "well-known," và "surroundings") phù hợp với ngữ cảnh và chủ đề của bài viết. Tuy nhiên, để tăng cường tổng thể về Lexical Resource, bạn có thể cố gắng sử dụng thêm một loạt từ vựng và cấu trúc câu phức tạp để làm cho bài viết của bạn trở nên đa dạng hơn.
