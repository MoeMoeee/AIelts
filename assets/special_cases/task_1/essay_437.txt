Please find below the edited version of your essay according to the IELTS structure:

The two maps illustrate an industrial area in the town of Norbition, as well as the planned future development of the site.

In the first map, a river can be seen running from the west to the east in the northern part of the field. On the north side of the river is farmland, while on the opposite side lies the industrial area. Positioned in the center is a roundabout with two exits, both of which connect to the main roads. To the west of the industrial area, there is a town.

Moving on to the second map, a new exit can be observed to the north, which connects to the road situated in front of several houses. Towards the west of the field, a few houses and shops are planned to be constructed. Meanwhile, in the eastern part of the field, a playground is set to be built. Additionally, a medical center and a school application are proposed to be established to the east of the medical center.

By analyzing these two maps, it becomes apparent that with the advancements in technology and the application of social development, significant changes can be made to the town and the small field. Furthermore, the industrial area has the potential to be transformed into a remarkable space.
