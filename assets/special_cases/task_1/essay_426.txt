Overall, it is clear that the recycling of aluminum consistently increased throughout the entire period, while the recycling of paper and glass saw only marginal increases. The recycling of plastic waste remained relatively stable.

Overall, the recycling of aluminum stood out as the most successful and exhibited a consistent upward trend. On the other hand, the recycling rates for paper, glass, and plastic waste were comparatively lower, with only minimal changes over the given period.
