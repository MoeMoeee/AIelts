Following this, the milk is transported to the main factory, where it undergoes a lengthy process that leads to the production of pasteurized milk and other dairy products such as cheese, cream, and butter. 
These products are then distributed extensively to supermarkets and shops. 
In conclusion, milk production involves a series of steps to ensure the production of high-quality milk and various dairy products.
