Topic:

"The course director of your previous college has invited you to attend a party for new students, and he has also asked you to give a talk about studying overseas. You have an important examination on the same day so you cannot attend the party.

Write a letter to the director to apologize and to explain why you cannot attend the event. Suggest another date when you could give Your talk."

Essay:

"Dear Mr. Loyds,

Thank you for your kind invitation to attend the party for new students on the 29th of January. Thank you also for thinking of me to give a talk. However, unfortunately I am unable to attend the party.

I am afraid that on the following day, I have my first semester economics exam. This is a complicated subject and a good mark is essential for me, so I have to spend the day before studying. I asked my lecturer if itwould be possible to take the examination on another date, but this is not permitted.

However, I would be very happy to give the talk on another day. I am available on the 5th and the 6th of February. If one of those dates is convenient for you, please let me know. I can be contacted at the address above or fax or telephone.

I hope that everyone at the college is well, and I hope that your party is a great success. I look forward to hearing from you.

Yours sincerely, Amy Sanilar"


Revised:

Dear Mr. Loyds,

I am writing to express my sincere appreciation for the invitation to attend the party for new students on the 29th of January and for the opportunity to give a talk. However, I regret to inform you that I will be unable to attend the event.

Regrettably, I have an important examination scheduled for the following day, which is my first semester economics exam. As this subject is quite complex, I believe that dedicating the day prior to the exam to study and preparation is crucial for achieving a satisfactory grade. Despite my efforts, it is not possible for me to reschedule the examination, as per the rules and regulations of the college.

Nonetheless, I am more than willing to give the talk on an alternative date that is convenient for both of us. I am available on either the 5th or the 6th of February. Kindly inform me of your preference, and I can be reached at the address mentioned above, as well as via fax or telephone.

I sincerely hope that the party will be a resounding success and that the new students will receive a warm and welcoming experience at the college. Please extend my regards to the college community and convey my apologies for my absence.

Thank you for your understanding, and I eagerly await your response regarding the rescheduled date for my talk.

Yours sincerely,

Amy Sanilar
