Revised:

Here is the edited version of the essay following the IELTS structure:

"The maps illustrate the developments that occurred on an island before and after the construction of tourist facilities.

Overall, there was a transformation from an uninhabited area to a destination with tourist amenities.

Initially, the island was completely empty and devoid of any tourist infrastructure. However, there was a beach present, although swimming access was not available. Subsequently, the layout of the island experienced significant changes. Firstly, accommodations were constructed on both sides, providing living quarters for visitors. This was complemented by footpaths for pedestrians. Towards the upper part of the island, a restaurant was built, while the reception area was located lower down, surrounded by a vehicle track. This track extended all the way to the pier, that was installed on the lowermost section of the island where boats could be docked. On the left side of the accommodations, a paved footpath led directly to the beach, which was also enclosed with a designated swimming area.

In conclusion, the two maps depict a remarkable shift from an unoccupied island to one that boasts various tourist amenities. This development not only includes the construction of accommodations, restaurants, and reception areas but also the establishment of footpaths and swimming areas."
