Revised:

In this modern era, opinions about the value of time and money vary among individuals. Some people contend that having more money and less leisure time is superior to having a low income and abundant free time. From my standpoint, while having more free time can offer some advantages, earning a higher income brings more benefits in numerous ways.

Firstly, while having more free time allows people to focus on self-care and improving their spiritual well-being, having a low income and too much time can be dull and unrewarding. Money is essential for meeting basic needs and living a comfortable life. Without sufficient funds, people cannot afford to socialize with friends or pursue their interests, leading to stress and dissatisfaction. Additionally, as the Covid-19 pandemic continues to impact the economy negatively, fresh graduates may find themselves in a difficult situation if they are unable to secure employment. Having too much free time can also lead to negative emotions and thoughts, as people may begin to compare their lives to others who are more successful.

On the other hand, having more money and less free time can be beneficial in various ways. For instance, some individuals who earn a higher income may choose to spend significant time with their family to make up for the time they spend at work. They can also enjoy the finer things in life by paying for superior services, such as high-quality travel or dining experiences. Although they may not have as much free time as those with lower incomes, the time they do have together with their loved ones is meaningful and cherished.

In conclusion, having more money and less free time can be advantageous in many ways. People who earn a higher income are often motivated to work hard and prioritize their time to balance work and personal life. This approach brings prosperity and convenience to their lives, allowing them to appreciate their precious leisure time more fully.

Grammatical Range and Accuracy:

Bài viết của bạn có một số lỗi ngữ pháp, tôi sẽ liệt kê và giải thích chi tiết dưới đây:

1. "Because value of money bring us more benefits." 
   Sửa thành: "Because the value of money brings us more benefits."
   Giải thích: Sau từ "because", cần sử dụng một cấu trúc câu đầy đủ, bao gồm một chủ ngữ (the value of money) và động từ (brings).

2. "Although people can spend more time to take care of themselves or enhance value of spiritual fife, having life with the low income and too much time is boring." 
   Sửa thành: "Although people can spend more time taking care of themselves or enhancing the value of their spiritual life, having a life with a low income and too much time is boring."
   Giải thích: Cần sử dụng dạng nguyên thể của động từ sau "spend more time" (taking care), và cần điền "their" trước "spiritual life" để chỉ người đang nói.

3. "We can not hang out with friends or enjoy own pleasure and it is an easy way which leads stress."
   Sửa thành: "We cannot hang out with friends or enjoy our own pleasure, and it is an easy way that leads to stress."
   Giải thích: Cần sử dụng "our" thay vì "own" để chỉ người đang nói, và cần sử dụng "that" thay vì "which" để kết nối câu.

4. "And in period when Covid-19 impact negatively on the national economy, senior student which have just graduated could land in an impasse if they don’t find a job."
   Sửa thành: "And in a period when Covid-19 impacts negatively on the national economy, senior students who have just graduated could land in an impasse if they don’t find a job."
   Giải thích: Cần sử dụng "a" trước "period" để chỉ một khoảng thời gian
