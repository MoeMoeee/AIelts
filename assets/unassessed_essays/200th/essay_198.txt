Topic:

"Countries are becoming more and more similar because people are able to buy the same products anywhere in the world.
Do you think this is a positive or negative development?
Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience."

Essay:

"With developing of the world's connection and the ability to access any production regardless of the location of a person, the becoming of some countries similar to each other is not a new topic to be argued. Regarding this phenomenon, I think it is a positive developmend due to the decreasing of discrimination level and formation of one "nation".

First and foremost, having an opportunity to acquire the same stuff notwithstanding the location will lead to the declining of discrimination in the world. By this, I mean that access of anyone to the products that are trended in developed countries will not make people from the third-world places be marginalized. If everyone's appearance is quite the same, people will have less reasons to judge each other. For instance, nowadays, a plethora of famouse brends , be it Bershka or Zara, have their product around the world. Thus, people do not have to travel to America to buy a new pair of jeans from H&M because they have it in their own country.

Another point to consider is that being similar to each other can form one intiger society. If all of the countries have quite the same way of wearing clothes, consuming products, it can lead to the reaching of one of the Sustainable Development Goals formated by UNESCO. The main idea of SDG is to be Global Citizens, since we are living in(on) one planet. Therefore, people should not think that they are belonging to one particular country and think beyond the borders. If those happen, people can solve any problem that currently occures in the world.

In conclusion, I am convinced that purchasing of the same products all over the world which makes more contries have less (fewer) differences between each other can be seen as a positive development. In conseqence, this leads people to not discriminate and be one unit."

Revised:

In today's globalized world, it has become easier for people to purchase the same products regardless of their location. This has resulted in some countries becoming more and more similar to each other. In this essay, I will argue that this phenomenon is a positive development as it leads to a decrease in discrimination and the formation of a cohesive global society.

One significant advantage of people being able to access the same products is that it leads to a decrease in discrimination based on appearance. When everyone has access to the same products, they tend to look and dress similarly. Therefore, people are less likely to judge or discriminate against someone because of their appearance. For example, popular brands such as Bershka and Zara have their products available worldwide, which means that people from third-world countries can also wear trendy clothes without feeling excluded. In this way, the availability of products creates a level playing field, decreasing the discrimination level across the world.

Moreover, the homogenization of products consumed globally can lead to the formation of a cohesive global society. If all countries have access to the same products, it can promote a sense of unity and shared identity. This aligns with one of the Sustainable Development Goals laid out by UNESCO, which is to create global citizens who think beyond borders and work together to solve problems. When people feel like they are part of one integrated society, they are more likely to work towards a common goal, and solve global issues such as climate change, economic disparity and cultural differences.

In conclusion, the homogenization of products consumed globally is a positive development. It leads to a decrease in discrimination and fosters the formation of a cohesive global society. By embracing this shared global identity, we can work together to tackle various global issues.

Grammatical Range and Accuracy:

Đánh giá chi tiết về Grammatical Range and Accuracy trong bài viết của bạn:

1. "...the becoming of some countries similar to each other is not a new topic to be argued."
=> "The fact that some countries are becoming increasingly similar to each other is not a new topic of debate." 
- Sử dụng "the fact that" thay vì "the becoming of".
- Thay đổi trật tự của các từ để câu trở nên rõ ràng hơn.

2. "Regarding this phenomenon, I think it is a positive development due to the decreasing of discrimination level and formation of one "nation"."
=> "Regarding this phenomenon, I think it is a positive development due to the decrease in discrimination levels and the formation of a global community."
- Sử dụng "the decrease in" thay vì "the decreasing of".
- Thêm "a" trước "global community" để chỉ sự hình thành một cộng đồng toàn cầu.

3. "By this, I mean that access of anyone to the products that are trended in developed countries will not make people from the third-world places be marginalized."
=> "By this, I mean that when anyone has access to products that are popular in developed countries, people from third-world countries will not feel marginalized."
- Thêm "when" để câu trở nên dễ hiểu hơn.
- Sử dụng "popular" thay vì "trended".

4. "If everyone's appearance is quite the same, people will have less reasons to judge each other."
=> "If everyone's appearance is quite similar, people will have fewer reasons to judge each other."
- Sử dụng "similar" thay vì "the same".
- Sử dụng "fewer" thay vì "less" vì "reasons" là số đếm được.

5. "For instance, nowadays, a plethora of famouse brends , be it Bershka or Zara, have their product around the world."
=> "For instance, nowadays, a plethora of famous brands, be it Bershka or Zara, have their products sold around the world."
- Đổi "brends" thành "brands".
- Thay "product" thành "products" để phù hợp với số nhiều.

6. "If all of the countries have quite the same way of wearing clothes, consuming products, it can lead to the reaching of one of the Sustainable Development Goals formated by UNESCO."
=> "If all countries adopt similar ways of clothing and consumption, this could contribute to achieving one of the Sustainable Development Goals set out by UNESCO."
- Tách câu thành hai câu nhỏ để tránh dài và bừa bộn.
- Sử dụng "adopt similar ways" thay cho "have quite the same way" để câu trở nên trơn tru hơn.
- Sử dụng "contributing to achieving" thay vì "leading to reaching".

7. "The main idea of SDG is to be Global Citizens, since we are living in(on) one planet."
=> "The main idea of the SDGs is to promote the concept of global citizenship, acknowledging that we all live together on this planet."
- "SDGs" (Sustainable Development Goals) là số nhiều, cần thêm "s".
- Thêm từ "promote the concept of" để nêu rõ ý nghĩa hơn.
- Thay "be" thành "acknowledging" để câu trở nên mạch lạc hơn.

8. "If those happen, people can solve any problem that currently occures in the world."
=> "If this happens, people can work together to solve any problem that currently occurs in the world."
- Sử dụng "this" thay vì "those" để tránh gây nhầm lẫn.
- Thêm "work together to" để câu trở nên cụ thể hơn.
