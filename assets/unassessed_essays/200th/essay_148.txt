Revised essay:

Advertising serves as a primary source of customer engagement for companies to promote their products. While some individuals argue that these promotions entice us to buy things we don't require, others contend that they inform us about new items that can improve our lives. In my opinion, I agree with both perspectives, and in the following paragraphs, I will explain why.

On the one hand, advertisements can seem deceptive as they use persuasive language to attract consumers and push them to buy products that they might not need. Companies do this to increase their sales figures, and the impact can be seen in various industries, such as cosmetics. For example, the cosmetic brands have introduced several products that claim they are distinct from other items, while in reality, there is little difference between them. They target each age group and gender specifically, not to meet the customers' needs but to attract them to their products. As a result, the companies capitalize on advertisements' power to manipulate consumers to achieve their sales goals.

On the other hand, advertising can be informative and helpful, as it can introduce consumers to new products that can enhance their lifestyles. For example, a new medical technology device can save someone's life, and if people were not made aware of it through advertising, they might not know about it. Additionally, some advertisements offer discounts or bonuses, which can be beneficial to individuals. For instance, supermarkets often provide offers that allow customers to purchase a product and get another one for free.

In conclusion, the influence of advertising on consumers is undeniable. However, it can be for better or worse, depending on one's perspective. On the one hand, advertising can be manipulative and encourage us to purchase items unnecessarily. On the other hand, advertising can inform us about new products that can improve our lives. To address any negative impacts, regulators must scrutinize advertisements more closely and, as consumers, we should make conscious and informed choices when purchasing products.
