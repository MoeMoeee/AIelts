"Some people say History is one of the most important school subjects. Other people think that, in today’s world, subjects like Science and Technology are more important than History. Discuss both these views and give your own opinion"

Essay:

"Technology is flourishing by leaps and bounds and provide us avenues whilst making our lives easy and comfortable. Therefore, many people tend to believe that academic subject Science and Technology must be given too-notch position rather than history because the past doesn't hold any implication in today life. Revised:

Technology is advancing rapidly, providing us with various means of making our lives more comfortable and convenient. Consequently, some individuals believe that academic subjects such as Science and Technology are more crucial than History in today's world since the past holds no significant significance in our current lives. While I partially agree with this perspective, I believe that both subjects are equally critical. In this essay, I will discuss the importance of both subjects and present my opinion.

On the one hand, technology is ubiquitous in all aspects of life. Appliances such as washing machines and light bulbs are products of scientific inventions that have made our lives easier. Additionally, scientists conducted research during the pandemic to produce an antidote that saved numerous lives. Furthermore, it is a rapidly growing field with vast career prospects.

On the other hand, learning about our past is essential for humanity's betterment. Our roots, culture, and past beliefs are critical in shaping our identity and understanding where we come from. The lessons from past events or conflicts can help us avoid repeating the mistakes of our ancestors. For example, the mass destruction of people during World War II serves as a lesson to respect everyone, regardless of their race, religion, or gender. These values are essential to maintaining social harmony and peace.

In conclusion, science and history are both vital academic subjects that have their strengths. Science has made our lives simpler and presented numerous career possibilities, whereas history contributes to our morals, cultural roots and identity. It is crucial to allow students to choose which subject to study based on their interests and career goals rather than prioritizing one over the other.
