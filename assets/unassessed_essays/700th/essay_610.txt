There are also online stores that allow people to earn significant sums of money each month. Furthermore, in times of disaster, the media can quickly convey information and help can be provided in the time of need. Indeed, the media has some positive effects on human life as well.

In conclusion, I believe that every invention has both positive and negative impacts, and it ultimately depends on how individuals use it.
