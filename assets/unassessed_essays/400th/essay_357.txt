Topic:

"it is generally believed that some people are born with certain talents for instance for sports or music and others not.Discuss both view and your opinion"

Essay:

"Some believe talent is the priority for success, but others believe talent does not play a role in success. Also, education and perseverance are more important than that. I agree with both groups.

All human beings are born with different talents, which can be said that a large part of them is transmitted through genetics, but it cannot be said that it includes all cases. Many talents of every human being are unknown and should be known through experience in different fields. If you know your talents, you can quickly develop them and be more creative.

Others believe in the beneficial effect of education. If you do not have any knowledge or talent in various fields such as music or art, with regular effort and good and educated teachers, you have a greater chance of success. Although trying can be much more challenging, its effect is more permanent. Frequent and regular effort can be much better than empty talent because you know and strengthen your weaknesses with practice.

In short, talent plays a significant role as long as you know them. The following condition is attractive, automatically making you practice or develop yourself, and these three things will lead to progress. Like Homayoun Shajarian, who, in addition to his talent and interest in music, his father's teachings and other teachers made him practice for years, and finally, after many years, he released his album and surprised everyone."


Revised:

Some people believe that talent is the key factor for success, whereas others argue that talent alone is not enough and that education and perseverance are more crucial. I agree with both viewpoints.

It is undeniable that individuals are born with certain innate talents. These talents are often believed to be influenced by genetics, although this may not be the case for everyone. Many talents are often discovered through personal experiences and exposure to various fields. Knowing your talents can enable you to nurture them more effectively, leading to enhanced creative abilities.

On the other hand, there are those who emphasize the importance of education. They contend that even if one lacks natural talent in areas such as sports or music, consistent efforts and guidance from well-educated teachers can greatly increase the likelihood of success. While this may require more arduous exertion, its effects are often long-lasting. Committing to regular and focused practice is often more advantageous than relying solely on innate talent, as it helps identify and strengthen areas of weakness.

In conclusion, talent does play a significant role in achieving success, provided one is aware of their talents. This awareness serves as a driving force, motivating individuals to dedicate themselves to practice and self-improvement. Moreover, a combination of talent, educational opportunities, and persistent efforts is more likely to lead to progress. A noteworthy example is the Iranian musician Homayoun Shajarian, who not only possessed a natural talent and interest in music, but also benefited from the teachings of his father and other mentors. Through years of dedicated practice under their guidance, he surprised everyone with the release of his album.
