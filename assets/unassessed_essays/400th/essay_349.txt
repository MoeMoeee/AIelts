Topic:

"In some countries, more and more people are becoming interested in finding out about the history of the houses or building they live in. 

What are reasons for this? 

How can people research this?"

Essay:

"There is a growing number of people who start to care about the history of their houses, flats or apartments in numerous nations nowadays. In the following essay, I will discuss what may contribute to this phenomenon and how people gain information about it.

To begin with, there are several factors that have responsibility for the society’s increasing interests in the history of the buildings where they live in. Firstly, house plays an important role in maintaining us keeping away from dangerous, in other words, house ensures our safety. As a result, it is of importance for individuals to understand that what had happened in the past before they choose the house or flat that they are going to live in. Secondly, with the knowledge of their houses and buildings, it is possible to make the residents to be proud of their community and become willing to participate in public decision, and enhance a sense of community.

As for the ways to gain an insight into the research, the elderly and the Internet are the two main resources to get information. From the aspect of the old, they have experienced a lot of experiences, and also have a possibility to suffer from some accidents that happened several years ago. If people want to acquire the first information about the house, building or accommodation, having an interview with elderly is a good manner. Searching on the Internet is another way to learn about the history, and it is also an efficient way.

	In conclusion, knowing the history of our houses or buildings enables us to enforce the sense of community, furthermore, it can help the society to be more careful to make a choice before buying houses, flats or departments."


Revised:

In several countries, there is a growing interest among people in exploring the history of the houses or buildings they reside in. This essay will discuss the reasons behind this trend and suggest ways in which people can conduct research on the subject.

There are various factors contributing to the increasing curiosity about the history of residential properties. Firstly, understanding the historical significance of a house is essential for ensuring personal safety. Knowing the past incidents and events associated with the property allows individuals to make informed decisions when selecting a place to live. Secondly, a knowledge of the history of their houses or buildings fosters a sense of pride and belonging within the community. This pride can result in increased civic participation and a more cohesive neighborhood.

To conduct research on the history of their dwellings, individuals can rely on two primary resources: the elderly and the internet. The elderly possess a wealth of personal experiences and anecdotes that can shed light on the past. By interviewing older members of the community, one can gather valuable information about the historical context of a house or building. Another effective method is conducting online research. The internet provides a convenient and efficient means of accessing historical records, public archives, and articles related to the specific property.

In conclusion, exploring the history of our houses or buildings serves to strengthen community bonds and enables individuals to make informed decisions when purchasing or choosing a place to live. This can be achieved through learning from the wisdom of the elderly and utilizing the resources available on the internet. By understanding the past, individuals can forge a stronger sense of connection to their homes and neighborhoods.
