Firstly, the Internet provides a vast amount of information on various topics, including those covered in museums. By doing so, he not only saved money and time but also avoided potential fatigue.

In conclusion, the abundance of information available on the Internet and the potential savings of time and money support the belief that museums are no longer necessary. Consequently, I agree with the view that people do not need to visit museums anymore.
