It is essential for parents, teachers, and law enforcement agencies to provide guidance and support to help teenagers understand and correct their actions during this delicate period.

In my opinion, the implementation of curfews is vital and should be enforced in all countries. This measure serves as a significant deterrent against the development of negative habits among teenagers. By imposing curfews, a significant decrease in the overall crime rates of regions can be anticipated. To effectively enforce this law, the government should impose heavy fines on both the teenager and their parents who violate the curfew, as strict law enforcement remains the best solution to prevent teenagers from engaging in undesirable actions.

In conclusion, while some may have reservations about curfews targeting teenagers, I am in favor of this initiative. Enforcing curfews during specific hours can help mold a stronger character amongst teenagers. By instilling discipline and discouraging negative behaviors, curfews are a forceful tool to maintain the well-being of young individuals and reduce crime rates in society.
