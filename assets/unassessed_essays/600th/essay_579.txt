Topic:

"Traffic and accomodation problems are increasing and government should encourage some businesses to move from cities to rural areas. Do the advantages outweigh the

 disadvantages?"

Essay:

"The increasing number of vehicles and housing problems have caused officials to motivate businesses to relocate from major cities to the countryside. Whilst acknowledging that there are drawbacks, this essay will argue that the advantages of this change will outweigh the disadvantages.

To begin with, the predominant advantage of the moving away of companies is definitely the development of the countryside. To further explain this, the relocation of major companies to the countryside is going to increase the labour force which would in turn help in economic growth leading to more education and employment opportunities, hence causing the rural areas to develop. Another major advantage of the relocation of businesses would be reduced pollution levels in major cities. With the reverse migration to rural areas, there would be a reduced number of automobiles leading to less carbon footprint and decreasing greenhouse effect. For instance, The major city in India, Delhi, is ranked as the most polluted city in the country, because of  overpopulation and huge traffic problems. Thus, the economic and environmental aspect of the relocation of businesses is highly advantageous.

While the aforementioned pros, there are several negative aspects to this that still need to be considered. One of the biggest drawbacks is the cost and time it would require to travel to work. People who live in the centre would now have to change their lifestyle and commute almost 30 to 40 kilometres every day to the job which would require not only plenty of time but also would cost a lot of money too, leading to elevated stress levels. For example, a research study done by  Harvard University showed that stress levels were more in an individual whose work was far away from home as compared to the one who loved nearby his job. 

To conclude, Although there are numerous benefits and drawbacks of moving businesses to the rural side at the same time,  the economic and  environmental benefits of the relocation of businesses are tremendous. Hence, the positive effects of  moving companies away definitely outweigh the negative ones."


Revised:

The increasing number of vehicles and housing problems have prompted officials to encourage businesses to relocate from major cities to the countryside. While there are some drawbacks to this, this essay will argue that the advantages of this change will outweigh the disadvantages.

To begin with, the primary advantage of businesses moving away from cities is the development of the countryside. Relocating major companies to rural areas will increase the labor force and contribute to economic growth, leading to more education and employment opportunities. This will facilitate the development of rural areas. Another major advantage of business relocation is the reduction in pollution levels in major cities. As more businesses move to rural areas, there will be a decrease in the number of automobiles, reducing the carbon footprint and mitigating the greenhouse effect. For instance, Delhi, the major city in India, is ranked as the most polluted city in the country due to overpopulation and traffic problems. Therefore, the economic and environmental benefits of business relocation are substantial.

Despite the aforementioned benefits, there are several negative aspects that need to be considered. One of the biggest drawbacks is the cost and time required for commuting to work. People living in the city center would have to change their lifestyles and commute up to 30 to 40 kilometers every day to their jobs. This would not only consume a significant amount of time but also incur additional transportation expenses, leading to elevated stress levels. For example, a research study conducted by Harvard University showed that individuals who had to travel far to work experienced higher stress levels compared to those living closer to their workplaces.

In conclusion, while there are numerous benefits and drawbacks to relocating businesses to rural areas, the economic and environmental advantages outweigh the negative aspects. Therefore, the positive effects of moving companies away from cities are significant.

Grammatical Range and Accuracy:

Trong bài viết của bạn, có một số lỗi nhỏ về ngữ pháp. Dưới đây là danh sách những lỗi sai và giải thích cách sửa chúng:

1. "Whilst acknowledging that there are drawbacks, this essay will argue that the advantages of this change will outweigh the disadvantages." 
   - Gợi ý sửa: Dùng "outweighed" thay vì "will outweigh".
   - Giải thích: Sử dụng thì hiện tại đơn ("outweigh") để miêu tả những lợi ích vượt trội so với những bất lợi là một quy tắc ngữ pháp thông thường.

2. "To further explain this, the relocation of major companies to the countryside is going to increase the labour force which would in turn help in economic growth leading to more education and employment opportunities, hence causing the rural areas to develop."
   - Gợi ý sửa: Dùng "helps" thay vì "would" trong mệnh đề quan hệ "which helps in economic growth".
   - Giải thích: Để thể hiện mối quan hệ nguyên nhân và kết quả, chúng ta sử dụng thì hiện tại đơn ("helps") thay vì thì tương lai ("would") trong trường hợp này.

3. "Another major advantage of the relocation of businesses would be reduced pollution levels in major cities."
   - Gợi ý sửa: Dùng "reduction" thay vì "reduced".
   - Giải thích: "Reduction" là danh từ cần thiết để miêu tả lợi ích của việc giảm mức độ ô nhiễm.

4. "People who live in the centre would now have to change their lifestyle and commute almost 30 to 40 kilometres every day to the job which would require not only plenty of time but also would cost a lot of money too, leading to elevated stress levels."
   - Gợi ý sửa: Loại bỏ "would" trước "require".
   - Giải thích: "Require" đã đủ để diễn tả điều kiện và không cần thêm "would" trong cấu trúc này.

5. "For example, a research study done by Harvard University showed that stress levels were more in an individual whose work was far away from home as compared to the one who loved nearby his job."
   - Gợi ý sửa: Dùng "lived" thay vì "loved" và đặt "near" trước "his job" để biểu thị vị trí.
   - Giải thích: "Lived" là động từ quá khứ của "live" và phù hợp hơn với ngữ cảnh của câu. "Near" được dùng để chỉ khoảng cách vị trí.
