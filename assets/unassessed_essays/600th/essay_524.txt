Topic:

"Nowadays, many people have to work longer hours, and they feel more stressed out than before. What are the reasons? What can employers do to make their life easier?"

Essay:

"It is undeniable that affording life expenses become a significant issue among people, and this phenomenon has affected mental health. This essay will examine the roots and possible solutions to maintain this concern.



On the one hand, There are several reasons for people struggling with nervousness due to financial barriers. First of all, It is evident to everybody that working conditions have become more complicated, and this situation has led to confined employees to their job in order to keep their job secure. Furthermore, life expenses have become a principal dilemma among folks. They should afford the cost of rent, installments, or even unforeseen expenses like disease treatment. Hence, these causes play a key role in employees' mental health.



It is worth mentioning that employers could act as a vital role in combating the devastating disorder. For instance, employers can offer some promotions based on employees' competency. Employers can consider a program for employees in which they can raise their salary if they have some proposals in order to enhance company income. On the other hand, employers can consider a break time for rest each hour or even provide some gaming counselors in order to have moments of tranquility and vitality with colleagues. Therefore, there are several opportunities that employers can offer to cease employees' mental issues.



To draw a conclusion, it should be reiterated that even though financial barriers are an inseparable dilemma in this hectic life, employers could act as a vital role in alleviating this detrimental disorder."


Revised:

It is undeniable that the growing pressure to afford life expenses, combined with increasingly demanding work hours, has led to heightened levels of stress among individuals. This essay aims to explore the underlying causes of this issue and suggest potential solutions for employers to alleviate the burdens faced by their employees.

On one hand, there are several reasons why individuals are experiencing elevated stress levels due to financial constraints. Firstly, the work environment has become more complex and challenging, forcing employees to dedicate more time and effort to their jobs in order to secure their positions. Additionally, the rising cost of living, encompassing expenses such as rent, installments, and unexpected bills like medical treatments, serves as a significant challenge. Consequently, these factors significantly contribute to the deterioration of employees' mental well-being.

On the other hand, employers have the ability to play a crucial role in addressing this debilitating issue. For example, employers can introduce promotion schemes based on employees' competencies and performance. By implementing programs that reward employees for proposing ideas to enhance company income, employers can foster a sense of value and motivation among their workforce. Furthermore, employers can encourage regular breaks and provide recreational activities or designated spaces for relaxation during work hours. These initiatives not only allow employees to recharge but also foster a sense of tranquility and vitality amongst colleagues. Consequently, employers possess multiple avenues to address the mental health concerns of their employees.

In conclusion, while financial barriers are an inherent challenge in today's fast-paced society, employers have the capacity to alleviate the negative impact on individuals' mental well-being. By implementing measures such as promotions that recognize employees' competence and creativity, as well as providing opportunities for rest and relaxation, employers can effectively contribute to relieving their employees' stress levels.
