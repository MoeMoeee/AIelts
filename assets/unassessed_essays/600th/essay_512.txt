Topic:

"Environmental problems such as pollution and climatic variations are increasing nowadays. The governments have taken some measures at a global level. But they got only few solutions. Why is it so? How can this be solved?"

Essay:

"Pollution and climate change are going up every day. Moreover, governments are offering solutions that are not effective on a worldwide scale. In this essay, I will explain the reasons behind this and provide some potential solutions.



If we compare the world now to 100 years ago, we can identify the problem. In the past, people lived in rural areas and consumed what they grew. However, after the industrial revolution, more communities migrated to cities and their habits shifted from being producers to consumers. Companies now strive to sell their products in order to increase their wealth, and governments do not have the authority to limit them, so even in developed countries this problem remained as it was. Additionally, the media industry and the internet assist companies in reaching customers worldwide. Resulting in increased use of fossil fuel products delivery. For instance, wooden types of furniture can produce in any country, but for the sake of advertisements, imported type is used.



Another issue is the encouragement of excessive consumption. In the past one car was sufficient for a family, but now it has become common for each person to own their own car. With the increasing human population on Earth, the need for food is undeniable. As a result, organizations are employing conservation and other production techniques to produce and store food for longer periods of time or cutting trees of jungles to build construction for industrial reasons. However, this leads to the use of more industrial machines, facilities and pesticides in agriculture. While there are benefits to these practices, associations often use excessive amounts of water and pollute it before returning to nature. For example, in my country, one of the most well-known companies is a major contributor to water pollution as they release dirty water into rivers, and the government cannot do anything about it.



In summary, these changes are inevitable, but humans are accelerating them. The only way to slow down these changes is by teaching people to love the Earth like other creatures in the world."


Revised:

"Environmental problems such as pollution and climatic variations are increasing nowadays. The governments have taken some measures at a global level, but they have only achieved limited success. In this essay, I will explain the reasons behind this and propose potential solutions.

To begin with, one of the main reasons for the ineffectiveness of government measures is the rapid industrialization and urbanization that has occurred in the past century. In the past, people lived in rural areas and were self-sufficient, consuming what they grew. However, with the advent of the industrial revolution, more communities migrated to cities and became dependent on mass production and consumption. Companies are driven by profit motives and governments often lack the authority to restrict their activities. As a result, even in developed countries, pollution and environmental degradation remain significant challenges. Furthermore, the advancement of media and the internet has enabled companies to reach consumers globally, leading to increased transportation and delivery of goods, which further contributes to pollution and climate change.

Another contributing factor is the culture of excessive consumption that has emerged in contemporary society. In the past, one car would suffice for a family, but now it has become common for each individual to own their own vehicle. Moreover, with the growing global population, ensuring an adequate food supply has become essential. This has led to the adoption of intensive agricultural practices, such as the use of pesticides and heavy machinery, as well as deforestation to create more land for agricultural purposes. While these practices have certain benefits in terms of increasing food production, they also result in excessive water consumption and pollution. For instance, in my country, a prominent company is known for polluting rivers with its release of untreated wastewater, yet the government seems powerless to address the situation effectively.

In conclusion, the current environmental problems we face are a result of human activities that have been accelerated by globalization and the culture of excessive consumption. To address these challenges, individuals must be educated about the importance of environmental preservation and sustainability. Governments should also strengthen regulations and policies to limit the harmful impacts of industrial activities and encourage more sustainable practices. Additionally, international cooperation and agreements are essential to tackle global environmental issues. Only by fostering a collective sense of responsibility and promoting sustainable development can we hope to overcome the environmental challenges we face."

Lexical Resource:

Trong bài viết của bạn, có một số từ vựng và cụm từ phổ biến và tiêu chuẩn. Tuy nhiên, có một số lỗi và cách sử dụng từ không hoàn toàn chính xác. Dưới đây là danh sách một số lỗi sai và giải thích:

1. "going up" - Thay thế bằng "increasing". "Going up" là một ngôn ngữ hội thoại, trong khi bạn nên sử dụng ngôn ngữ chính thức trong bài viết IELTS.

2. "communities migrated to cities" - Thay thế bằng "people moved to cities". "Communities migrated to cities" có vẻ khá hình thức và không phù hợp trong bối cảnh này.

3. "strive to sell their products" - Thay thế bằng "aim to sell their products" hoặc "make efforts to sell their products". "Strive to sell" có nghĩa gốc là "nỗ lực bán hàng", tuy nhiên trong ngữ cảnh này, các công ty không chỉ nỗ lực bán hàng mà còn cố gắng bán hàng để tăng doanh số.

4. "for the sake of advertisements, imported type is used" - Thay thế bằng "due to advertising purposes, imported types are used". "For the sake of" có nghĩa là "vì lợi ích của...". Trong khi đó, trong ngữ cảnh này, việc sử dụng những loại nhập khẩu có liên quan đến mục tiêu quảng cáo.

5. "encouragement of excessive consumption" - Không có lỗi sai trong việc sử dụng cụm từ này. Tuy nhiên, bạn có thể sử dụng một cụm từ giống nghĩa khác để tránh lặp lại: "promotion of overconsumption".

6. "the need for food is undeniable" - Sử dụng "the demand for food is undeniable". "Need" có nghĩa là "nhu cầu", trong khi "demand" phù hợp hơn với ngữ cảnh trong câu này.

7. "organizations are employing conservation and other production techniques" - Thay thế bằng "organizations are adopting conservation and other production techniques". "Employ" có nghĩa là "tuyển dụng" và không phù hợp với ngữ cảnh trong câu này.

8. "associations often use excessive amounts of water and pollute it before returning to nature" - Thay thế bằng "associations often excessively use water and pollute it before releasing it back into the environment". "Before returning to nature" không chính xác trong ngữ cảnh này, chỉnh sửa câu để phù hợp với nghĩa.

Về tổng thể, bài viết của bạn vẫn chứa rất nhiều từ vựng và cụm từ phù hợp và trình bày ý kiến một cách rõ ràng. Tuy nhiên, hãy chắc chắn sử dụng từ ngữ và cấu trúc câu chính xác và phù hợp trong bài viết.


Grammatical Range and Accuracy:

Trong bài viết của bạn, tôi đã nhận thấy một số lỗi sai về ngữ pháp. Dưới đây là danh sách các lỗi và giải thích:

1. "Pollution and climate change are going up every day." -> "Pollution and climate change are increasing every day." or "Pollution and climate change are on the rise every day." (Sử dụng cấu trúc động từ chính đúng).

2. "Companies now strive to sell their products in order to increase their wealth, and governments do not have the authority to limit them, so even in developed countries this problem remained as it was." -> "Companies now strive to sell their products in order to increase their wealth, and governments do not have the authority to limit them. As a result, even in developed countries, this problem has remained unresolved." (Sử dụng cấu trúc câu hoàn chỉnh và đảm bảo sự liên kết logic giữa các ý).

3. "Resulting in increased use of fossil fuel products delivery." -> "Resulting in an increased use of fossil fuel products for delivery." (Thêm mạo từ "an" để bổ sung ngữ cảnh cho câu).

4. "The need for food is undeniable. As a result, organizations are employing conservation and other production techniques to produce and store food for longer periods of time or cutting trees of jungles to build construction for industrial reasons." -> "The need for food is undeniable. As a result, organizations are employing conservation and other production techniques to produce and store food for longer periods of time, as well as cutting down trees in jungles for industrial construction purposes." (Sử dụng dấu phẩy để tách các ý và sử dụng các cụm từ chính xác).

5. "However, this leads to the use of more industrial machines, facilities and pesticides in agriculture." -> "However, this leads to the increased use of industrial machines, facilities, and pesticides in agriculture." (Sử dụng từ "increased" để chỉ sự gia tăng và thêm dấu phẩy cho các danh từ cách nhau).

6. "While there are benefits to these practices, associations often use excessive amounts of water and pollute it before returning to nature." -> "While there are benefits to these practices, associations often use excessive amounts of water and pollute it before it is returned to nature." (Thêm đại từ quan hệ "it" để sự liên kết và sự chính xác trong câu).

7. "For example, in my country, one of the most well-known companies is a major contributor to water pollution as they release dirty water into rivers, and the government cannot do anything about it." -> "For example, in my country, one of the most well-known companies is a major contributor to water pollution because it releases dirty water into rivers, and the government cannot do anything about it." (Sử dụng từ "because" để thể hiện nguyên nhân rõ ràng).
