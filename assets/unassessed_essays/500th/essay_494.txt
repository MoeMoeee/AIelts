Topic:

"Many students find it difficult to pay attention at school. What are the reasons for this? What could be done to solve this problem?"

Essay:

"Due to increasing pressure and various other circumstances, children nowadays find it challenging to engage and concentrate on their academics at school. However, there are certain measures that can be taken to resolve this issue and make learning more fun and enjoyable for students. 

Primarily, the lack of attention is due to the traditional teaching techniques followed by institutions. Passive teaching is sure to bore students rather than develop their talents since each student's grasping ability differs. The teachers are instructing students to memorise the answers instead of engaging in brainstorming and creative learning activities. The students rely on their memory and write the answer in the examination, which becomes the reason for frustration among the students. The use of portable devices such as mobile phones during lectures is another source of distraction. Instead of focusing on academics, students spend the majority of their time playing video games and surfing the internet. 

Alternatively, if instructors in schools use active learning techniques, in which each student is given the opportunity to exhibit what they have learned, students' learning will increase. Teachers should also use a variety of instructional strategies to pique students' interest and engagement, such as presenting videos, engaging through presentations, and personally contacting students. Finally, because distractions like video games and cell phones are the most prevalent cause of a child's lack of interest in studying, they should be banned in schools, and parents should impose a daily usage restriction on them. 

To sum up, modern teaching techniques should be implemented in order to develop students' enthusiasm for learning and gadgets should be prohibited and limited because they tend to act as a barrier and reduce students' effectiveness"


Revised:

Many students find it difficult to pay attention at school due to various reasons. One of the main causes is the traditional teaching methods used in schools. Passive teaching often bores students and fails to develop their talents, as each student's learning style and ability differs. Instead of engaging in brainstorming and creative activities, students are instructed to memorize answers, which leads to frustration. Additionally, the use of portable devices, such as mobile phones, during lectures is a major source of distraction. Students often spend their time playing video games or browsing the internet, diverting their attention from academics.

To address this issue, it is important for schools to adopt active learning techniques. By giving each student the opportunity to demonstrate their understanding of the subject matter, learning engagement can be increased. Teachers should also utilize a variety of instructional strategies, such as incorporating videos, interactive presentations, and personalized communication with students. This can help to capture their interest and encourage active participation.

Moreover, it is crucial to reduce the influence of distractions like video games and cell phones within the school environment. Schools should impose a ban on these devices to minimize their interference. Furthermore, parents should also play a role in limiting the usage of such devices at home and enforcing a daily restriction.

In conclusion, the lack of attention among students can be improved by implementing modern teaching techniques that foster enthusiasm for learning. Additionally, restricting and banning the use of distracting gadgets like video games and mobile phones can enhance students' focus and effectiveness in their studies.
